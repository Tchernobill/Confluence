## Physical Description

**Build:** 5'10", average male frame with large shoulders  
**Appearance:** Dark brown hair (unkempt, falls across face), dark brown eyes, pale skin from underground living  
**Hidden Features:** Multiple precise surgical scars, subtle cybernetic ports, callused hands from technical work  
**Enhancement Markers:** Virus-enhanced healing, strength, and reflexes (carefully concealed)  

---

## Personality

### Core Traits
- **Arc 1:** Introverted, brilliant, traumatized, caring but guarded
- **Later Arcs:** Strategic, protective, mysteriously capable, charismatic, burdened by secrets

### Philosophy & Values
- **Core Belief:** "Once I know how it works, it can be fixed"
- **Moral Code:** Universal balance (yin-yang), secular humanism, everyone deserves second chances
- **Authority View:** Deeply suspicious of corporate power, believes in earned authority through competence

### Psychological Profile
- **Greatest Fear:** Hurting loved ones, discovery of enhanced nature
- **Greatest Joy:** Successfully protecting people without them knowing the true cost
- **Coping Mechanisms:** Sleeps in confined spaces, uses technical metaphors
- **Blind Spots:** Difficulty maintaining genuine connections due to hidden nature

---

## Abilities & Powers

### Public Capabilities
- Master-level electronics repair and modification
- Exceptional tactical awareness and timing  
- Business management and strategic planning
- Appears to have supernatural intuition about technology

### Secret Enhancements (Virus-Enhanced)
- **Physical:** Enhanced reflexes, strength, healing factor
- **Mental:** Enhanced cognitive processing and memory, pattern recognition
- **Technical:** Electromagnetic manipulation, universal electronic hacking
- **Advanced:** Near-telepathic connection to electronics, nanobotic control
- **Limitation:** Must carefully moderate responses to appear within normal human range

## Story Arc Progression

### Arc 1: The Hidden Survivor
- **Status:** Isolated scavenger living in ventilation shaft
- **Challenge:** Surviving while hiding extraordinary abilities
- **Growth:** Learning to trust others (Isa, Zara, Jin)

### Arc 2-3: The Reluctant Business Owner  
- **Status:** Phoenix Recovery founder, emerging leader
- **Challenge:** Building legitimate business while managing secret
- **Growth:** Developing leadership skills, expanding influence

### Arc 4-6: The Benevolent Authority
- **Status:** Territory protector, mythical figure
- **Challenge:** Balancing power with humanity
- **Growth:** Accepting responsibility for others' lives and futures
