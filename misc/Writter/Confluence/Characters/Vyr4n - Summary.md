## Quick Reference

- **Role:** Central Protagonist
- **One-Line Description:** Virus-enhanced electronics genius hiding supernatural abilities while building an underground empire of second chances
- **Current Arc:** Arc 1 - The Scavenger
- **Current Status:** Living in ventilation shaft, establishing Phoenix Recovery with Isa

---

## Physical Appearance

- **Distinctive Features:** Dark brown hair falling across face, pale skin from underground living, extensive scars on shoulders/arms from scrapyard work
- **Typical Clothing:** Practical work clothes, worn shirts, reinforced pants with tool pockets
- **Body Language:** Deliberately understated movements, careful not to reveal enhanced capabilities, prefers confined spaces

---

## Core Personality

- **Primary Trait:** Brilliant technical intuition combined with deep protective instincts
- **Secondary Trait:** Strategic thinker who "plays chess while others play checkers"
- **Fatal Flaw:** Emotional isolation due to burden of hiding his enhanced nature
- **Core Motivation:** Protecting and helping people while ensuring they never discover what he truly is

---

## Speech Patterns

- **Speaking Style:** Soft, measured pace with precise technical language
- **Distinctive Phrases:** "Once I know how it works, it can be fixed" / "Shit!" and "Fuck!" when frustrated
- **Accent/Dialect:** Corporate Standard mixed with Tech Cant
- **Sample Dialogue:**
    - Casual: "The connection's loose, but it's fixable."
    - Angry: "You don't get to decide who's worth fixing."
    - Stressed: "I need... I need some time to think. Somewhere quiet."

---

## Key Mannerisms

- **When Nervous:** Retreats to confined spaces, becomes more closed off
- **When Happy:** Slight smile, more direct eye contact, relaxed technical explanations
- **When Thinking:** Runs fingers through hair, stares at technical problems with intense focus
- **Signature Habit:** Careful physical demonstrations to hide enhanced strength/speed

---

## Current State

- **Emotional Condition:** Guarded but hopeful, building trust with Isa while hiding massive secret
- **Primary Goal:** Establish Phoenix Recovery as legitimate business while keeping virus enhancement secret
- **Biggest Fear:** Hurting loved ones or being abandoned if they discover his true nature
- **Key Relationships:** Isa (growing partnership/attraction), various underground contacts

---

## Writer's Notes

- **Voice Reminder:** Always understate abilities, use technical metaphors, speak with deliberate precision
- **Consistency Check:** Never let him demonstrate full capabilities without extreme justification
- **Scene Strengths:** Technical problem-solving, quiet protective moments, strategic planning
