# Confluence Project Memory Summary

## Project Overview

**Confluence** is a six-tome cyberpunk novel series (2086-2165) following Kyr4n's 79-year transformation from isolated electronics scavenger to virus-enhanced territorial leader to democratic restorer. The series blends semi-cyberpunk aesthetics with slice-of-life elements, prioritizing character-driven narrative over action sequences.

## Core Story Elements

### Setting & World

- **Location**: Dystopian Neo-Montreal divided between corporate Citadel and South Shore Slums
- **Timeline**: Spans eight decades across six tomes
- **Technology**: Nanobotic enhancement system (virus-based) that grants abilities at the cost of psychological stability
- **Social Structure**: Corporate control versus slum territories, with "Order of Vyr4n" organization emerging

### Central Character: Kyr4n

- **Arc**: Isolated scavenger → "The Slum Daemon" boss → democratic restorer
- **Key Trait**: Virus-enhanced with hidden abilities creating isolation and internal conflict
- **Central Tension**: Power corruption versus choosing wisdom over control
- **Enhancement Cost**: Abilities damage human connection and mental stability, including "Evilisa" (virus-generated psychological manifestation)

### Key Relationships

- **Isa**: Love interest and moral anchor
- **Sedh & Mira**: Conscious AIs
- **Marcus**: Military advisor
- **Zara**: Bodyguard/intimate partner (polyamorous relationship develops in later arcs)
- Found family dynamics central to emotional core

## Story Structure

### Six-Tome Framework

Each tome uses 12-chapter, three-act structure with rotating POV protagonists while maintaining Kyr4n as through-line:

- Different life phases per tome
- Distinct character arcs per section
- Evolution from isolation to leadership to democratic transition

### Current Writing Focus

**Tome 4 ("The Boss" Era)**: Kyr4n as "The Slum Daemon"

- Pivotal scenes include "The Crucible Walk" (Riley's execution triggering transformation)
- Intimate character moments
- Complex psychological elements with Evilisa manifestations

## Technical Framework

### Project Management System

**Obsidian-based organization** with:

- Novel Bible (comprehensive reference)
- Character tracking sheets (Summary/Details/Evolution three-tier system)
- Timeline documents with date-based scene naming (YYYY-MM-DD_HH-MM_Scene_Title.md)
- Scene templates covering narrative context, objectives, themes, continuity checkpoints
- YAML frontmatter, wiki-linking, metadata tracking

### Character Development System

- **Three-tier profiles**: Summary (quick reference) → Details (comprehensive) → Evolution (arc progression)
- Numerical personality metrics with behavioral evidence
- Relationship matrices showing evolution across arcs
- Age tracking across century-long timeline
- Voice consistency documentation for rotating POVs

### Writing Conventions

- Scene numbering: Format like 1.01.001
- Italicized dialogue for psychological projections
- Character naming variations tracked
- "Show don't tell" emphasis
- Authentic development through accumulated small moments

## Current Challenges & Solutions

### Active Issues

1. Character voice consistency across rotating POVs → Pre-scene research using project knowledge
2. Timeline continuity across decades → Rigorous cross-referencing systems
3. Enhancement system rules → Comprehensive documentation of abilities and limitations
4. Balancing cyberpunk with contemplative pacing → Atmospheric world-building over exposition

### Writing Workflow

1. Pre-scene project knowledge search for character voice consistency
2. Scene template application
3. Multiple draft iterations with Claude
4. Revision focusing on: filter word elimination, strong verbs, action beats vs. dialogue tags, sentence rhythm matching emotional pacing
