Excellent points! Both additions would be highly relevant and useful. Here's how to incorporate them:

## **For Character Type/Archetype:**

Add this to the **Quick Reference** section:

```markdown
## Quick Reference
- **Role in Story:** [Protagonist/Antagonist/Supporting/Minor]
- **Character Archetype:** [The Mentor/The Rebel/The Innocent/etc.]
- **Story Function:** [Comic Relief/Voice of Reason/Catalyst/etc.]
- **Narrative Type:** [Dynamic/Static/Round/Flat]
```

This helps you quickly understand each character's narrative purpose, especially useful when juggling 20 characters.

## **For Multi-Decade Development:**

Replace the simple Story Integration section with a **timeline-based approach**:

```markdown
## Story Integration - Arc

### Character Timeline

#### **[DECADE/YEAR] - Age [X]**
- **Life Stage:** [Child/Teen/Young Adult/etc.]
- **Physical Changes:** [Notable aging, health changes]
- **Psychological State:** [Maturity level, worldview]
- **Key Relationships:** [Who matters most in this period]
- **Major Events:** [What happens to them]
- **Core Conflict:** [What they're struggling with]
- **Skills/Knowledge:** [What they've learned/lost]
- **Sample Dialogue:** "[How they speak at this age]"

#### **[DECADE/YEAR] - Age [X]**
- **Life Stage:** 
- **Physical Changes:** 
[Continue for each major time period...]

### Arc Summary
- **Overall Journey:** [Child to elder, innocent to cynical, etc.]
- **Major Transformations:** 
  - Physical: 
  - Psychological: 
  - Social: 
  - Spiritual: 
- **Constants:** [What never changes about them]
```

## **Additional Tracking Tools:**

Consider creating a separate **Character Evolution Matrix** spreadsheet with:
- Character names as rows
- Time periods as columns  
- Key changes in each cell (appearance, beliefs, relationships, status)

This gives you a bird's-eye view of all 20 characters aging together.

**Pro tip:** Note which characters' archetypes shift over time (The Innocent becomes The Cynic, The Sidekick becomes The Mentor) as this can create powerful story moments.
