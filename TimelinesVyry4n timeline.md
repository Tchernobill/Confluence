- **Location**: [[South Shore Slum]]
- **Family**: Single mother (bio-tech engineer), father unknown
- **Context**: Born into post-collapse cyberpunk dystopia
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[World Events Timeline#^2086-slum-formation]]

### **2086-2102 (Ages 0-16)** - Childhood ^childhood-era
- **Event**: Raised by mother in underground maintenance tunnels
- **Education**: Self-taught electronics through mother's journals and salvaged manuals
- **Skills Developed**: Basic electronics, survival instincts, technical intuition
- **Personality Formation**: Learns to hide vulnerabilities, values independence
- **Related Events**:

- **Targets**: Oppressive corporate leadership, systemic exploitation
- **Justification**: Protecting people requires destroying the systems oppressing them
- **Cost**: Further moral compromise, growing isolation from humanity
