## The Events

- Corporate enforcer in standard security gear approaches during busy afternoon period
- Claims "routine inspection" while scanning Vyr4n's equipment with detection devices
- Fabricated accusations about "unlicensed modifications" and "illegal enhancement technology"
- Demand for immediate "compliance fees" and future "protection payments"
- Vyr4n responds diplomatically, neither confirming nor denying accusations
- Vendor community implements subtle warning protocols and information sharing
- Enforcer increases pressure with veiled threats about "facility closure" and "equipment confiscation"
- Vyr4n maintains calm professionalism while assessing enforcer's capabilities and backup
- Enforcer leaves with promises of "future compliance visits" and escalated scrutiny

## Details to observe

- Corporate enforcer's professional intimidation techniques and fabricated authority
- Vyr4n's careful word choice avoiding both submission and direct confrontation
- Vendor community's subtle solidarity: casual positioning, information sharing, silent support
- Underground market's established protocols for handling corporate pressure
- Enforcer's scanning equipment designed to find violations regardless of actual compliance
- Financial pressure tactics designed to make underground operations unprofitable
