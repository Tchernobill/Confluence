## The Events

- Corporate security mech enters market for routine vendor harassment
- Escalating intimidation tactics against several vendors
- Unknown market participant throws EMP grenade at mech
- Electromagnetic pulse disables mech and surrounding electronic systems
- Immediate corporate backup called as security protocols activate
- Market vendors and customers begin panicked evacuation
- Communication systems failing across underground network
- Reports of spreading civil unrest throughout South Shore Slum
- Kyr4n and Isa caught in initial evacuation wave
- Both recognize this as sector-wide crisis, not isolated incident

## Details to observe

- Sudden transformation from routine business to life-threatening crisis
- Corporate enforcement response protocols creating escalation cycle
- Underground market community solidarity during crisis
- Electronic systems vulnerability to EMP weaponry
- Kyr4n's analytical assessment vs. emotional protective response
- Isa's street-smart crisis recognition and survival instincts
