## Scene Information

- **Summary**: Isa investigates "The Artisan" through her network of street contacts, gathering stories about his impossible repairs and fair pricing before deciding to approach him with a test job.
- **POV Character(s)**: Isa (primary)
- **Timeline**: 2108 - Several days after Chapter 1, Vyr4n age 22
- **Location(s)**: Various underground locations throughout South Shore Slum
- **Key Events**: Information gathering about Vyr4n's reputation, decision to approach with military-grade implant, preparation of test challenge
- **Foreshadowing Events**: Hints at Isa's extensive dealer network, establishes her methodical approach to partnerships
- **Character Developments**: Shows Isa's competence, caution, and business acumen; establishes her professional network
- **Plot Advances**: Sets up the catalyst for Vyr4n and Isa's relationship; establishes Isa as worthy business partner
- **Objects/Items Introduced**: Military-grade memory implant (test job), Isa's network contacts, underground information systems
- **Scene Goal**: Establish Isa as competent professional and create anticipation for first meeting
- **Scene Mood/Tone**: Professional curiosity, calculated assessment, growing intrigue
- **Transition to Next Scene**: Isa prepared and confident to approach "The Artisan" with challenging test job

## The Events

- Isa visits Chen at the food distribution area, asking casual questions about skilled repair specialists
- Contact with underground medic Dr. Sarah Kim, who mentions impossible neural interface repairs
- Meeting with electronics dealer Tommy "Splice" Martinez, learning about "The Artisan's" fair pricing and mysterious methods
- Observation from distance at underground market, watching Vyr4n work on precision repairs
- Private analysis of damaged military-grade memory implant, planning test approach
- Final decision and preparation for direct contact

## Details to Observe

- Isa's methodical approach to gathering information before making business decisions
- Underground network dynamics showing community of mutual support and information sharing
- Stories about Vyr4n's exceptional abilities told from different perspectives
- Contrast between Isa's social connectivity and Vyr4n's isolation (foreshadowing)
- Military-grade implant complexity establishing stakes for test job

**Foreshadowing Elements:**

- Isa's network will become crucial for Phoenix Recovery business expansion
- Military-grade technology hints at future corporate conflicts
- Community respect establishes foundation for later territorial protection
- Professional partnership approach sets pattern for organizational loyalty development
