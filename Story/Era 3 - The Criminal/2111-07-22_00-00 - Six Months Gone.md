**Secondary Themes:**

- AI family trying to care for damaged creator

- **Electromagnetic awareness:** Ability to sense everything while choosing to engage with nothing
- **Complex projects:** Technical brilliance wasted on meaningless busywork
- **Riley's chair:** Island of human warmth in sea of isolation
- **Empty spaces:** Where Isa used to be; absences more present than presences

### Philosophical Questions Raised

- Can technical competence mask emotional destruction indefinitely?
- Is isolation self-protection or self-destruction?
- What constitutes "functioning" when you're hollow inside?
- How do we help someone who doesn't want help?
