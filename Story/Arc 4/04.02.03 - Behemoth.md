## Scene events

# Scene Summary - Key Events

- Vyr4n working on supply manifests when Rodriguez bursts in with urgent news
- Rodriguez reports Behemoth is rampaging through their territory
- Marcus reacts with helpless dismay ("Fuck") at facing this impossible threat
- Vyr4n contrasts this with Rico's old passive approach to Behemoth rampages
- Vyr4n decides to act personally, takes Zara despite Marcus suggesting coordinated response
- They arrive at market square to find three bodies: Maria Santos, her son Carlos, and enforcer Jenkins
- Vyr4n confronts Behemoth, state he would had offered peaceful resolution
- Behemoth finds the idea amusing
- But Vyr4n add that he killed people that were under his protection, he just can't have that
- Behemoth focus his full attention on Vyr4n
- Vyr4n strips to basic clothes, hands equipment to Zara who protests
- Marcus offers sniper support via comm, Vyr4n refuses - he's bored and need some action
- Civilians gather at safe distance, some recording and livestreaming the confrontation
- Behemoth attacks first with hydraulic-powered strikes, Vyr4n evades and counters tactically
- Extended fight with Behemoth's overwhelming power against Vyr4n's precise positioning
- Vyr4n appears to be losing and is getting progressively more injuried, but systematically gathering data on Behemoth's attack patterns
- Vyr4n uses tactical positioning to vault onto Behemoth's back, locking legs around neck and arms around his head
- Vyr4n presses hands to Behemoth's head with concentrated expression
- Behemoth screams in agony, blood seeps from his eyes, then collapses backward
- Vyr4n struggles free from under Behemoth's corpse, injured but alive
- Vyr4n gives cover story about massive enhancements system overheating... massively
- Civilians disperse, spreading word and sharing recordings through underground networks
- Zara questions the explanation, Vyr4n stick to his cover story, Zara doesn't belive it
- They return to compound while cleanup crew handles aftermath
- Zara realizes the impossible victory will build Vyr4n's legend as the mysterious "Slum Daemon"
