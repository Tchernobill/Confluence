## The Events

- Sun low over South Shore as Kyr4n walks through abandoned industrial district
- He spots large waste disposal building (incinerator design)
- Enters building, light changes from amber to diffuse bands through ventilation slits
- Moves through entrance corridor into main processing area
- Begins reading the building like a machine: pipes, valves, conduits, junction boxes
- Meditative state settles over him—communion with systems, not people
- Descends deeper, light fading, switches to flashlight
- Reaches basement levels where only his footsteps echo
- Experiences complete isolation and peace in the building's depths
- Systematically searches basement, finding occasional useful components
- All senses awake: flashlight beam, cool air, chemical ghosts, echoes building mental map
- His mind works at center, understanding the building's original function as incinerator
- Flashlight catches reflection—glass/optical sensor
- Navigates through debris pile between fallen beams
- Excavates and discovers android head in excellent condition
- Examines it carefully: high-grade metal, intact sensors, sophisticated construction
- Recognizes it as professional-grade, modular design, potential project
- Feels unfamiliar flutter—curiosity, possibility
- Wraps head in cloth, places in backpack
- Retraces path through basement, up stairs, through corridors
- Emerges at dusk, stars visible
- Pauses to acknowledge the building before leaving
- Walks back to underground room, mind already working on the puzzle
