## The Events

- Kyr4n arrives at underground market after tunnel journey
- Sets up portable workstation in his usual corner alcove between support pillars
- Positions hand-lettered sign and displays examples of previous work
- First client arrives: nervous woman with damaged data pad (flickering screen)
- Performs routine repair: loose display cable connection, five minutes work
- Receives payment of 50ml fresh water (easy money)
- Second client: implant calibration for enhancement software drift
- Third client: communication device repair for underground dealer
- Fourth client: targeting system modification for security guard with substandard equipment
- Mid-morning lull: brief conversation with Marcus (fence) passing by stall
- Marcus mentions recent shipment of quality components, offers Kyr4n first look
- More repair work through late morning: neural interface recalibration begins
- Early afternoon: Dr. Nyx stops by, discusses market conditions and corporate surveillance increase
- Continues work on complex neural interface requiring careful adjustment
- Additional clients throughout afternoon with various repair needs
- Each interaction follows pattern: assessment, estimate, careful work, untraceable payment
- "The Artisan" reputation reinforced through satisfied clients
- By end of day: several successful repairs, two ongoing projects requiring additional components
- Earns enough credits for week's basic expenses plus small margin for opportunities
- Packs up portable workstation efficiently
- Exchanges nods with regular vendors as market begins to close
- Prepares to leave for scavenging run in abandoned industrial district

## Details to observe

- **Underground market atmosphere**: Abandoned metro station repurposed, vendor stalls built from salvaged materials, harsh emergency lighting, electrical hum providing white noise, platforms connected by walkways over empty tracks
- **Kyr4n's strategic positioning**: Corner alcove between pillars offers good sight lines for security while remaining discrete, carefully chosen location balancing visibility and safety
- **Portable workstation details**: Folding surface that expands for work space but remains light enough to carry, everything designed for quick setup and evacuation if needed
- **Deliberate modesty**: Sign and displays intentionally understated—flashy success attracts predators, modest presentation keeps him safe while getting enough business
- **Two-tier service model**: Public repairs (appliances, simple devices) for everyone; advanced work (neural interfaces, targeting systems) only for those who know to ask
- **Professional competence**: His hands work with precision and intuition, almost meditative focus, understanding systems at deep fundamental level
- **Transaction patterns**: Brief technical assessment, honest estimates, careful work while client watches or steps away, payment in untraceable credits, no questions asked
- **"The Artisan" origin**: Nickname emerged organically from satisfied customers, spread through word-of-mouth referrals, reflects exceptional skill level
- **Economic survival**: Each successful repair builds toward sustainable existence, not prosperity but survival with small margin
- **Social dynamics**: Comfortable with transactional professional interactions, awkward with personal connection, vendor relationships based on mutual respect and business
- **Risk management**: Constant awareness of balance between visibility needed for business and invisibility needed for safety

**Character Development:**

- **Kyr4n**: Reveals his one area of complete confidence—technical work. When his hands are on electronics, he's not the isolated, wounded orphan but a skilled artisan who understands systems at their deepest level. The way clients call him "The Artisan" and the nickname has spread organically demonstrates earned respect in underground community. His comfort with transactional interactions but clear discomfort with anything personal establishes the emotional barriers that relationships (Isa, Sedh) will need to overcome.
    
- **Underground Market Community**: Introduced through Marcus and Dr. Nyx as regular vendors, showing that despite isolation, Kyr4n exists within a professional community based on mutual respect and business relationships. The market operates on trust, reputation, and discrete transactions—a shadow economy with its own rules and culture.
