## üìñ Scene Structure

### Opening Beat
**Anchor:** Training Room Alpha, afternoon, three days after "The Morning After"; Kyr4n being questioned by inner circle about capabilities
**Hook:** "Math doesn't work" - how does 70kg baseline beat augmented heavyweights?
**Mood/Atmosphere:** Curious, slightly challenging, professional setting with undercurrent of intimacy

### Key Story Beats (in sequence)
1. **The Question:** Marcus/Alex/Zara question Kyr4n's abilities; math doesn't work; he deflects; Zara calls bullshit on "good training" explanation ‚Üí Establishes need for demonstration
2. **Marcus's Request:** Direct ask to see capabilities for tactical planning; Spartan-style straightforward; "We have your back, understanding helps" ‚Üí Kyr4n weighs revelation vs. secrecy
3. **Agreement to Spar:** Kyr4n agrees to "mild demonstration" with Zara; professional boundaries maintained; playful wink (complicity); they square up ‚Üí Tension builds
4. **Heat Demonstration:** Testing jab, counter near ribs, air shimmers; "localized microwave emission"; Alex: "Walking microwave"; sarcasm from Kyr4n ‚Üí First capability revealed
5. **Electrical Demonstration:** Committed combination, counter near sternum, audible crack, blue-white light; Zara's muscles lock briefly; "much milder than combat" ‚Üí Second capability, more dangerous
6. **Explanation & Confession:** Against augmented: override implants; against baseline: taser or lethal; had abilities for years; "taken long time to learn control"; can't be taught ‚Üí Full context provided
7. **Trust Moment:** Zara squeezes shoulder (brief, supportive); his tension drains; Marcus sees progress ‚Üí Emotional beat, connection forming
8. **TURNING POINT - Full Demo Request:** Alex wants "no holding back" on combat dummy; gap between shown and field reality; Kyr4n resists; Zara: "trust us with truth, all of it"; word lands like blow ‚Üí He agrees
9. **Setup:** Alex configures adaptive progression (Level 1-12); wall display stats; Kyr4n warns stay back; they retreat to barrier ‚Üí Anticipation builds
10. **Transformation:** Marcus-taught stance dissolves to feral predator instantly; all training vanishes into surgical violence ‚Üí Shocking shift
11. **Escalation (Levels 1-6):** Palm strike with heat, electric shoulder, each success increases difficulty; display shows climbing energy: 2.3 kJ ‚Üí 131.7 kJ ‚Üí Group watches in awe
12. **Devastation (Levels 7-12):** 131.7 kJ (car crash energy); dummy moving faster; Kyr4n flows, dismantles; Level 11: 267.4 kJ; Level 12 MAXIMUM: 389.2 kJ ‚Üí Horror and understanding
13. **Finale:** Hand around head, electrical hum, sensors explode, tears head free; final palm strike, visible shockwave, torso caves; dummy collapses destroyed ‚Üí 23 seconds total
14. **Statistics:** Display shows Level 12, 15 strikes, 11 dodges, 403.6 kJ total; Zara: "Fuuuuuck" ‚Üí Shocked processing
15. **Collapse:** Kyr4n sways, transformation reverses, sheet-white, trembling; "I need food. Now."; Marcus catches him ‚Üí Vulnerability and cost revealed
16. **Metabolic Crisis:** Burns calories "like marathon in 30 seconds"; electromagnetic generation requires massive energy; Alex brings protein bars, Zara brings water; gentle care ‚Üí Understanding consequences
17. **Recovery:** Demolishes protein bars, color returns; explains frequency: once/twice daily maximum; push too far = muscle breakdown; emergency rations explained ‚Üí Limitations clear
18. **Understanding:** Marcus realizes food isn't paranoia; Alex: "you hide this every time"; group processes capability AND limitation ‚Üí Full picture
19. **Support:** Marcus: "not alone anymore"; Zara: gentle squeeze, stays close; Alex: logistics adjustments; group commits to protocols ‚Üí Found family solidifies
20. **Resolution:** Kyr4n agrees to protocols; Marcus arranges meals; truth shared completely; "We have each other. And the truth." ‚Üí Trust established

### Closing Beat
**Changed State:** Inner circle knows full capabilities and costs; Kyr4n accepted despite (because of) vulnerability; support systems established; isolation cracked
**Hook/Question:** What will he do with better control? How will this foundation support neural work with Zara? What crisis is coming?
