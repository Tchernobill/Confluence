## USAGE TIPS

1. **Keep Updated**: Update the bible with each writing session to ensure all new developments are captured.
    
2. **Track Character Evolution**: Pay special attention to how characters change across chapters.
    
3. **LLM Instruction**: When using LLMs, provide specific sections from the bible rather than the entire document.
    
4. **Consistency Checking**: Regularly compare new content against the bible to catch inconsistencies.
    
5. **Version Control**: Maintain dated versions of your bible as your story evolves.
    
6. **Searchability**: Use a digital format that allows for easy searching by character names, locations, etc.
    
7. **Visualization**: Consider supplementing with visual elements (character sketches, location maps, relationship diagrams).
    
8. **Cross-Reference**: Include chapter references for all important elements to easily find their first/key appearances.
    
9. **Living Document**: Treat the bible as flexible and evolving, not rigid and constraining.
    
10. **Regular Review**: Schedule periodic comprehensive reviews of the entire bible.
