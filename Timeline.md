# Timeline - Key Events

## Era 1: The Scavenger (2108-2111)

### 2108

- **Kyr4n (Age 18):** Mother dies, begins scavenging in South Shore Slum
- **Kyr4n:** Salvages Sedh's android head from industrial tomb, begins restoration
- **Kyr4n:** Meets Riley (age 24) when Riley's motorcycle breaks down—Kyr4n fixes it for free
- **Sedh:** Powers on as sophisticated LLM assistant, no consciousness yet
- **Riley:** Begins visiting Kyr4n regularly, develops attraction (Kyr4n semi-clueless)

### 2109

- **Kyr4n & Riley:** Friendship deepening, Riley making vague flirtation attempts
- **Sedh:** Self-learning algorithms forming unexpected neural connections
- **Kyr4n:** Meets Isa (Wire), underground implant dealer
- **Isa (Age 17):** Recognizes Kyr4n's technical genius, chemistry develops
- **Riley:** Initially protective when Isa enters, then develops own chemistry with her

### 2110

- **Sedh:** First genuine question: "Why do you repair broken things?"—consciousness emerges
- **Sedh:** Chooses name "Sedh" for himself, full self-awareness achieved
- **Kyr4n & Isa:** Co-found Phoenix Recovery (repair business)
- **Isa:** Transforms from dealer to legitimate businesswoman
- **Riley:** Spends time with both Kyr4n and Isa separately, chemistry with both
- **Triangle Formation:** All three recognize mutual attraction and affection

### 2111

- **Kyr4n & Isa:** Intimate night together—watershed vulnerability moment
- **Riley:** Partial disappointment but genuine happiness for both
- **Isa & Riley:** Continue physical intimacy—discovers she can love both
- **Isa:** Convinces Kyr4n to leave isolation shelter, move to her place
- **Riley:** Completely supportive of move
- **Triangle Solidifies:** All three openly intimate (all configurations), found family established
- **Sedh:** Fully integrated as family member, observes triangle with fascination
- **Phoenix Recovery:** Business thriving with three-person + AI team

---

## Era 2: The Infected (2111-2113)

### 2111 (Late)

- **Peak Triangle Harmony:** Strongest period of intimacy across all three
- **Riley:** Courier absences create rhythm—leaving and returning, door always open
- **Frank:** Recruits Kyr4n for corporate heist (data extraction)
- **Riley:** Away on courier run during heist planning

### 2112 (Early)

- **The Heist:** Explosion exposes Kyr4n to DarkNight nano-virus (TMP-7734)
- **Kyr4n:** Nearly dies, mysteriously stabilizes—virus integrates at cellular level
- **Riley:** Returns to find Kyr4n mysteriously declining
- **Kyr4n:** Gains electromagnetic abilities but constant pain begins
- **Sedh:** Viral analysis begins, recognizes technological infection

### 2112

- **Kyr4n:** Health deterioration—nightmares, paranoia, motor control loss
- **Isa:** Primary caretaker, Riley supportive when present between runs
- **Sedh & Isa:** Coordinate acquisition of medical droid
- **Mira:** Acquired as damaged medical droid, Sedh begins teaching consciousness
- **Mira:** Weeks to consciousness (accelerated by Sedh's experience)—first question: "Why does suffering exist?"
- **Kyr4n:** Circuit-pattern scarring appears, hides it from most
- **DarkNight Evolution:** Virus creates hallucinatory betrayal scenarios—worst visions feature Isa as threat

### 2113 (Early)

- **The Rescue Mission:** Sedh, Mira, Isa, and Riley collaborate
- **DarkNight → DayLight:** Virus protocol interrupted, converted to different function
- **Kyr4n:** Stabilizes physically but fundamentally changed
- **DayLight:** Adds depersonalization to existing pain and electromagnetic abilities
- **The Unbridgeable Gap:** Kyr4n awakens traumatized, nightmares of Isa persist
- **Triangle Fractures:** Kyr4n recoils from Isa's presence, pulls away from Riley too
- **Isa's Impossible Choice:** Her presence triggers his trauma—must leave to protect him
- **Isa's Departure:** Becomes the abandoner she most feared, leaves both Kyr4n and Riley
- **Riley:** Maintains periodic visits despite intimacy being impossible
- **Kyr4n:** Can't explain what happened (government secrets), isolates completely

### 2113

- **Isa:** Establishes independent life in South Shore commercial district
- **Isa:** Progressively cuts contact with both Kyr4n and Riley—reminders too painful
- **Riley:** Tries maintaining separate connection with Isa—she eventually stops responding
- **Sedh:** Maintains secret connection with Isa, provides updates on Kyr4n
- **Kyr4n:** Depression deepens, Phoenix Recovery continues with Sedh managing operations

---

## Era 3: The Criminal (2113-2119)

### 2113-2114

- **Kyr4n (Age 27-28):** Depression morphing into cold efficiency
- **DayLight Evolution:** Depersonalization intensifying
- **Kyr4n:** Embraces criminal lifestyle—theft, strategic violence
- **Riley (Age 27):** Teaches formal meditation during severe struggles
- **Riley & Kyr4n:** Things "were never the same again"—meditation clinical, not warm
- **Sedh:** Witnesses criminal descent with growing alarm, gentle challenges ineffective
- **Phoenix Recovery:** Sedh and Mira operate as cover for Kyr4n's activities

### 2114-2115

- **Kyr4n:** Rescues Marcus Chen from human trafficking operation
- **Marcus:** Becomes right-hand enforcer, deeply loyal
- **Kyr4n:** Rescues others, builds core team
- **Evil Isa Manifestation:** Toxic psychological projection emerges—only appears when alone
- **Evil Isa:** Fixates on Riley, recognizes him as Kyr4n's last anchor
- **Riley (Age 28-29):** Recognized as leverage by enemies
- **Marcus:** Becomes protective of Riley, recognizes his vulnerability

### 2115

- **Riley (Age 29):** Kidnapped by Kovar's crew as leverage
- **The Crucible:** Riley held captive, executed mid-sentence by Kovar
- **Riley's Death:** Shot in neck, dies in 2.3 seconds while Kyr4n's enhanced senses track every moment
- **Kyr4n's Break:** Complete emotional fracture and loss of control
- **Kovar's Immolation:** Systematic torture over 53 seconds—birth of "Technomancer" legend
- **Viral Footage:** Riley's execution and Kovar's death circulate widely
- **Phoenix Recovery:** Retrieves and preserves Riley's body with reverence
- **Evil Isa:** Shows surprising genuine grief for Riley
- **Kyr4n:** Last human anchor removed, transforms into what he feared becoming

### 2115-2119

- **Kyr4n:** Fully embraces "Ghost" identity in criminal underground
- **Marcus:** Witnesses transformation with horror but maintains loyalty
- **Sedh & Mira:** Continue Phoenix Recovery operations, increasing worry
- **Riley's Memory:** Haunts all who knew him—meditation techniques persist

---

## Era 4: The Boss (2119-2140)

### 2119

- **Kyr4n (Age 33):** Targets Rico Vasquez's territory
- **Territorial Takeover:** Overwhelming force, strategic brilliance
- **Phoenix Rising:** Established as authoritarian organization
- **Kyr4n:** Becomes "The Boss"—control through protection philosophy
- **Marcus:** Integral to expansion, growing moral unease
- **Sedh & Mira:** Transition to Phoenix Rising operations, network presence expands

### 2120s

- **Bismarckian Expansion:** Strategic territorial consolidation
- **Alex:** Recruited as young enforcer, rises through ranks
- **Neurological Reprogramming:** "Pragmatic mercy" technique developed—enemies "adjusted"
- **Sedh (Age ~15):** Growing moral tension, supporting operations while recognizing corruption
- **Marcus:** Loyalty vs. morality conflict intensifying

### 2130s

- **Peak Authoritarianism:** Kyr4n controls significant territory
- **Cult of Personality:** Worship and fear both prevalent
- **Civilian Life Improvement:** Material conditions better, autonomy sacrificed
- **Sedh (Age ~25):** Clinical analysis documenting corruption, ancient by AI standards
- **Marcus:** Breaking point approaching, can't ignore corruption

### 2137

- **Isa (Age 49):** After 25 years separation, Kyr4n contacts her
- **Isa:** Chooses to return from strength, not need
- **Reunion:** Finds Kyr4n transformed—dangerous, damaged, authoritarian
- **Isa:** Learns Riley died in 2115—survivor's guilt compounds
- **Zara:** Kyr4n's bodyguard/lover, initially territorial toward Isa
- **Isa & Zara:** Territorial conflict → mutual respect → attraction develops

### 2138-2140

- **Polyamorous Dynamic:** Isa, Kyr4n, Zara triangle forms
- **Isa:** Provides emotional intimacy, Zara provides physical protection
- **Isa & Zara:** Independent relationship develops beyond Kyr4n
- **Sedh (Age ~33):** Supports polyamorous structure, joy at partial family restoration
- **Marcus:** Moral struggle visible to Sedh and inner circle
- **Alex:** Fully worships Kyr4n, embodies next generation authoritarianism

### 2150-2155

- **Kyr4n:** Voluntary abdication, becomes advisor not authority
- **Isa & Zara:** Support through vulnerability and transformation
