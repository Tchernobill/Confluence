# Confluence Location Names Guide

## The Citadel (Montreal Island)

### Major Districts & Neighborhoods

- **Mary-Town** (Ville-Marie) - The historic core, now corporate headquarters
- **Crown Mountain** (Mont-Royal) - The fortified peak with defense arrays
- **West Peak** (Westmount) - Elite residential sector
- **Outer Mount** (Outremont) - Academic and research district
- **Royal Flats** (Plateau Mont-Royal) - Mid-tier residential, now corporate housing
- **Mile's End** (Mile End) - Former artist quarter, now data farms
- **Dragon Quarter** (Chinatown) - Preserved cultural district for executive entertainment
- **Heritage Quarter** (Old Montreal) - Luxury executive housing
- **Central Core** (Downtown) - Primary business and admin zone
- **Green Valley** (Verdun) - Manufacturing and worker housing
- **The Hall** (LaSalle) - Logistics and transport hub
- **The Machine** (Lachine) - Heavy industry and automated factories
- **Laurent Strip** (Saint-Laurent) - Entertainment and black market zone
- **High Flag District** (Hochelaga) - Security and enforcement sector
- **Mercer Ward** (Mercier) - Administrative processing
- **Rose Hill** (Rosemont) - Residential mid-management
- **Ray Town** (Villeray) - Surveillance and monitoring center
- **Hunter's Creek** (Ahuntsic) - Elite recreational facilities
- **Carter-ville** (Cartierville) - Transport and shipping
- **Leonard's District** (Saint-Léonard) - Corporate R&D facilities
- **Angel District** (Anjou) - Biotech and medical research
- **North Sector** (Montréal-Nord) - Waste processing and recycling
- **Prairie Banks** (Rivière-des-Prairies) - Water treatment and filtration
- **Tremor Point** (Pointe-aux-Trembles) - Power generation and heavy industry

### Landmarks & Infrastructure

- **The Observatory** (Mount Royal installations) - Defense command center
- **The Undergrowth** (Underground City) - Subterranean complex
- **The Sacred Heart** (Oratoire Saint-Joseph) - Abandoned spiritual site
- **The Bowl** (Olympic Stadium) - Converted data processing center
- **The Bell** (Bell Centre) - Corporate assembly hall
- **Crown Cross** (Mount Royal Cross) - Communications array disguised as monument
- **The Sphere** (Biosphere) - Environmental monitoring station
- **The Dame** (Notre-Dame Basilica) - Museum and tourist attraction for executives
- **Ram Castle** (Château Ramezay) - Historical archive and propaganda center
- **Good Help Market** (Marché Bonsecours) - Elite shopping complex
- **Arts Plaza** (Place des Arts) - Corporate entertainment complex

## Bodies of Water & Geography

### Major Waters

- **The Great River** / **Lawrence Flow** (Saint Lawrence River) - The primary barrier
- **Rich Flow** / **The Rich** (Richelieu River) - Southern water route
- **Prairie Stream** (Rivière des Prairies) - Northern water boundary
- **Saint Louis Lake** (Lac Saint-Louis) - Western water body
- **Prairie Basin** (Bassin de Laprairie) - Southern water collection

### Terrain Features

- **The Crown** (Mont-Royal) - The mountain fortress
- **The Heights** (Monteregian Hills) - Mining territory
- **The Flats** (Saint Lawrence Lowlands) - Industrial flatlands
- **The Divide** (Richelieu Valley) - Southern boundary region

## Bridges & Transportation

### Bridge Network

- **JeeCee Portal** (Jacques Cartier Bridge) - Primary checkpoint crossing
- **Champion Bridge** (Champlain Bridge) - Secondary military crossing
- **Victory Bridge** (Victoria Bridge) - Rail freight only
- **Mercer Span** (Mercier Bridge) - Western industrial crossing
- **The LaFont Tube** (Louis-Hippolyte-Lafontaine Tunnel) - Underwater cargo route

### Road Networks (Slang Names)

- **The Ten** / **Highway Dix** (Autoroute 10) - Eastern mining route
- **The Fifteen** / **North-South Corridor** (Autoroute 15) - Main supply line
- **The Twenty** / **East-West Main** (Autoroute 20) - Primary freight artery
- **The Thirty** / **South Bypass** (Autoroute 30) - Circumferential smuggling route
- **The Forty** / **Metro Highway** (Autoroute 40) - Cross-island route

### Underground Transit

- **The Underpass** (Yellow Metro Line) - Secret crossing tunnel
- **Blue Line** / **Deep Blue** (Blue Metro Line) - Citadel internal transport
- **Green Line** / **The Greenway** (Green Metro Line) - Main Citadel artery
- **Orange Line** / **The Orange** (Orange Metro Line) - Corporate executive transport

## Airports & Terminals

- **PET Terminal** / **Elliott Field** (Pierre Elliott Trudeau Airport) - Corporate air base
- **Saint-Hub Strips** (Saint-Hubert Airport) - Shore cargo and smuggling hub

## Corporate/Administrative Designations

### Official Sector Names

- **Alpha Sector** (Prime corporate zones) - Highest security areas
- **Beta Sector** (Secondary corporate) - Standard business districts
- **Gamma Sector** (Service areas) - Worker and service zones
- **Delta Sector** (Industrial) - Manufacturing and processing
- **Omega Sector** (Waste/abandoned) - Restricted or abandoned areas

### Security Zones

- **Red Zone** (Maximum security) - Corporate core and Crown Mountain
- **Yellow Zone** (Controlled access) - Standard Citadel areas
- **Green Zone** (Limited access) - Border and checkpoint areas
- **Black Zone** (No man's land) - Abandoned or dangerous areas
- **Blue Zone** (Water areas) - River and maritime zones
