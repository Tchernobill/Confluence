### **SCENE 6: "The Request" (Mind-Reading Request Scene)**

**Timeline Position:** After all trust-building scenes, BEFORE "The Crisis" **Chapter/Scene Code:** 4.06.004 or similar (before Crisis at 4.06.00X) **Primary POV:** Dual or alternating (both perspectives important) **Location:** Private location (his quarters, her quarters, or neutral secure space) **Time of Day:** Evening **Estimated Length:** 2,500-3,500 words

**Key Characters:**

- Zara (making request, reckless curiosity)
- Kyr4n (reluctant, knows dangers she doesn't)

**Scene Purpose:**

- Zara formally requests mind-reading
- Show her reckless confidence and complete trust
- Show his hesitation (knows dangers)
- Establish she's asking BEFORE knowing full danger
- Set up tension: request made, danger unknown, choice pending
- Create agreement to attempt it (with conditions)

**Key Beats:**

1. **Approach**: Zara initiates conversation about his abilities
2. **Direct question**: "Can you read minds?"
3. **His evasion attempt**: Tries to deflect, avoid answering
4. **Her persistence**: Won't let it go, pushes for real answer
5. **His admission**: Confirms it's possible but dangerous
6. **Her request**: "I want you to try it on me"
7. **His refusal**: Initial no, explains risk (vaguely)
8. **Her argument**: Complete trust, needs to understand herself
9. **His real fear**: "You don't know what you're asking"
10. **Her recklessness**: "Then show me"
11. **His conditions**: If they try, it's controlled, careful, he's in charge
12. **Agreement**: They'll attempt it, but he needs to prepare first
13. **Unspoken tension**: Both know this crosses line permanently

**Dialogue Flow:**

_Zara:_ "The interrogation with Wells. You did something that wasn't torture. Something... else."

_Kyr4n:_ "I extracted information. That's the job."

_Zara:_ "In seventeen minutes. Without laying a hand on him. Without raising your voice." (pause) "Can you read minds?"

_Kyr4n:_ "Zara—"

_Zara:_ "That's not a no."

_Kyr4n:_ "It's complicated."

_Zara:_ "Then uncomplicate it. Can you or can't you?"

_Kyr4n:_ (long pause) "Theoretically. Yes."

_Zara:_ "I want you to try it on me."

_Kyr4n:_ "Absolutely not."

_Zara:_ "Why not?"

_Kyr4n:_ "Because you don't understand what you're asking for."

_Zara:_ "Then explain it to me."

_Kyr4n:_ "It's... invasive. Dangerous. I could hurt you in ways that wouldn't show."

_Zara:_ "I trust you."

_Kyr4n:_ "You shouldn't. Not with this."

_Zara:_ "I've trusted you with my life. Multiple times. You've trusted me with yours. This is just..." (searches for words) "...a different kind of vulnerability."

_Kyr4n:_ "It's not the same—"

_Zara:_ "I need to understand myself. Why I pushed you to lose control. Why I wanted you to. What's broken inside me that needs..." (frustrated) "I can't figure it out alone. You can show me."

_Kyr4n:_ "And if what I find scares you?"

_Zara:_ "Then I'll finally know what fear feels like."

[Later, after more back-and-forth]

_Kyr4n:_ "If—and that's if—we try this... it's on my terms. Controlled. Careful. And we stop the second anything goes wrong."

_Zara:_ "Agreed."

_Kyr4n:_ "I need time to prepare. Make sure the framework is stable. This isn't something I do lightly."

_Zara:_ "How long?"

_Kyr4n:_ "Days. Maybe a week."

_Zara:_ "I can wait."

_Kyr4n:_ (looking at her seriously) "Zara... once I see inside your head... there's no unseeing it. For either of us."

_Zara:_ "Good. I want you to see."

**Mood/Tone:** Tense negotiation, underlying trust, her reckless determination vs. his cautious concern, intimate discussion of dangerous intimacy

**What This Sets Up:**

- Request made (commitment established)
- His preparation period (leads to modification attempt)
- Danger mentioned but not detailed (she doesn't know full risk)
- Agreement with conditions (controlled attempt)
- Timeline for "The Crisis" (his preparation goes wrong)
- Revelation significance (she'll learn what she's really asked for)
- Dark Maze inevitability (choice made, will follow through)
