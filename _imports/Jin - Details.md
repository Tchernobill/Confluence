> A slum-born tech prodigy whose illegal clinic nearly destroyed him, now finding redemption through ethical engineering under Kyr4n's mentorship

---

## Physical Appearance

### Basic Description
- **Height:** 5'6" (168cm)
- **Build:** Wiry and compact, optimized for crawling through tight spaces and precision work, surprisingly strong hands and forearms from years of precise work
- **Skin Tone:** Pale from years of underground work, slight yellow tint from workshop lighting
- **Hair:** Spiky black hair, usually disheveled from running hands through it while thinking
- **Eyes:** Dark brown, often hidden behind magnification goggles
- **Age Appearance:** Looks his age (26), though workshop isolation makes him seem younger in social situations

### Distinctive Features
- Multiple small scars on hands and forearms from tools and circuitry
- Burn mark on left palm from plasma cutter accident
- Small tattoo of circuit board pattern on right wrist
- **Scars/Marks:** Hands and forearms covered in technical work scars; each tells a story of a project or mistake
- **Tattoos/Modifications:** Circuit board tattoo is memorial to his first successful build

### Style & Presentation
- **Clothing Preference:** Oil-stained coveralls or work clothes, always wearing tool belts, fingerless gloves for dexterity
- **Grooming Habits:** Minimal - functionality over appearance; hair often messy from working
- **Accessories:** Safety goggles (pushed up on forehead when not working), multiple tool belts, fingerless gloves
- **Cybernetics/Augmentations:** Self-installed neural port for direct machine interface, enhanced vision implants for microscopic work, subdermal toolkit with basic electronics in left forearm

### Physical Mannerisms
- **Posture:** Slightly hunched from years of bent-over work, but straightens when excited about projects
- **Gait:** Quick, efficient movements; often distracted and needs to be called back
- **Distinctive Gestures:** Runs hands through hair when thinking, hums electronic music while working, talks to machines as if they're alive
- **Nervous Habits:** Organizes tools obsessively when stressed, fidgets with circuit components
- **Physical Tells:** Eyes light up when discussing technical problems; becomes animated despite usual awkwardness

## Personality & Psychology

### Core Traits
- **Dominant:** Perfectionist workaholic with absolute dedication to technical excellence
- **Secondary:** Socially awkward but becomes passionate and eloquent about technology
- **Tertiary:** Loyal to those who earn his respect, surprisingly brave when protecting work or friends
- **Hidden:** Deep insecurity and imposter syndrome despite obvious brilliance

### Strengths
- Brilliant technical mind with intuitive understanding of systems
- Innovative problem-solving abilities
- Perfect spatial reasoning and exceptional hand-eye coordination
- Absolute dedication once committed
- Surprisingly brave when it matters

### Weaknesses
- Obsessive tendencies that override self-care
- Poor social skills and emotional intelligence
- Physically fragile, limited combat ability
- Becomes impulsive when excited about new technology
- Neglects basic needs (sleep, food, relationships)

### Fears & Traumas
- **Greatest Fear:** Technology failing when lives depend on it
- **Deep Fear:** Being seen as inadequate or a fraud
- **Trauma:** Failed augmentation procedure that paralyzed a client (age 23)
- **Anxiety:** Returning to desperation of slum life
- **Social Fear:** Intimate relationships and emotional vulnerability

### Goals & Aspirations
- **Primary:** Perfect the art of human-machine integration
- **Personal:** Prove himself worthy of Kyr4n's mentorship and faith
- **Idealistic:** Create technology that genuinely helps people rather than exploits them
- **Professional:** Develop ethical augmentation procedures that set new standards
- **Hidden:** Find romantic connection despite social awkwardness

### Daily Quirks & Habits
- Hums electronic music while working
- Organizes tools with obsessive precision
- Lives on energy drinks and nutrient bars
- Talks to machines as if they're sentient
- Works 16-hour days routinely
- Collects vintage electronics and pre-collapse engineering texts
- Builds "unnecessary but elegant" gadgets in spare time

### Moral Philosophy
- Technology should enhance human potential, not replace human connection
- Believes engineering can solve human suffering
- Efficiency and beauty can and should coexist
- Second chances matter—people can be "patched" like broken systems
- Expertise must be earned through skill, not corporate authority

### Worldview
- Distrusts corporate authority but respects earned expertise
- Sees society as a broken machine that needs better engineering
- Believes in universal balance and second chances
- Technology as salvation and redemption

### Regrets & Joys
- **Greatest Regret:** Augmentation procedure that paralyzed a client; haunts him daily
- **Greatest Joy:** The moment a complex system comes online perfectly for the first time
- **Secret Joy:** When Kyr4n praises his work
- **Hidden Regret:** Never having normal social relationships

## Communication Style

### Speaking Patterns
- **Normal:** Rapid, technical speech when excited; uses engineering metaphors for everything
- **Under Pressure:** More technical jargon, speaks faster, gestures with hands
- **With Authority Figures:** Slightly deferential with Kyr4n but enthusiastic; awkward with others
- **With Peers:** Relaxes somewhat, uses more casual language but still tech-focused

### Language Profile
- **Vocabulary:** Technical and precise, engineering metaphors, casual profanity when stressed
- **Accent/Dialect:** Corporate Standard (fluent), Street Cant (native), Japanese (basic), Tech Shorthand, Machine Code
- **Rhythm:** Fast-paced when discussing tech, stumbling when discussing emotions
- **Formality Level:** Informal with colleagues, respectful but not formal with Kyr4n
- **Humor:** Dry, self-deprecating, often tech-related jokes
- **Argument Style:** Logic and data-driven; struggles with emotional arguments
- **Persuasion Style:** Demonstrates technical superiority through action
- **Lying Tells:** Becomes more technical and evasive; avoids eye contact even more than usual

### Sample Dialogue

GREETING: "Hey boss, got a minute? This neural pathway thing is fascinating."  
CASUAL: "I can make anything work better, faster, stronger—except my dating life."  
ANGRY: "This is sloppy work! The tolerances are off by microns—that could kill someone!"  
SCARED: "The system's cascading... I don't know if I can stop it..."  
PERSUADING: "Give me caffeine, components, and a deadline. I'll give you miracles."  
LYING: "The, uh, primary coupling matrix needs... recalibration of the flux capacitors..." [becomes overly technical]  
INTIMATE: "You make me want to be offline for a while. That's... that's new for me."

## Background & History

### Origins
- **Birthplace:** South Shore Slum, Underground Tech District—a warren of illegal workshops and salvage yards
- **Family Background:** Working class slum dwellers; grandfather was pre-collapse electronics technician
- **Cultural Context:** Third-generation slum life where technical skill was survival currency

### Family
- **Parents:** Died in factory explosion when Jin was 12
- **Siblings:** None
- **Extended Family:** Raised by grandfather (deceased) who taught him electronics
- **Family Dynamics:** Close bond with grandfather; isolated after his death
- **Family Legacy:** Grandfather's mechanical watch (pre-digital era) and technical knowledge

### Formative Years
- **Childhood:** Grew up in technical underground, learned electronics from grandfather
- **Education:** Self-taught through salvaged technical manuals and trial-and-error
- **Early Traumas:** Parents' death at age 12 forced him to survive through tech skills
- **Early Joys:** First successful circuit build; grandfather's pride in his abilities
- **Formative Relationships:** Grandfather as primary mentor and father figure

### Major Life Events
- **Age 12:** Parents killed in factory explosion—life-defining trauma
- **Age 16:** Built first successful augmentation—realized his calling
- **Age 19:** Opened illegal augmentation clinic in the slums
- **Age 23:** Disastrous procedure paralyzed a client—moral crisis point
- **Age 24:** Recruited by Kyr4n after impressing him with innovative repair work—redemption begins
- **Age 26:** Now Chief Engineer at Phoenix Recovery

### Defining Moments
- **Moment 1:** Grandfather showing him how circuits create beauty and function—sparked lifelong passion
- **Moment 2:** The failed augmentation that paralyzed a client—taught him the weight of responsibility
- **Moment 3:** Meeting Kyr4n and realizing his true potential with ethical guidance

### Secrets & Hidden History
- **Known Only to Them:** The specific details of the procedure that went wrong; nightmares about it
- **Known to Few:** Full extent of his illegal clinic operations and client list
- **Public vs Private History:** Known as brilliant engineer; not widely known how many illegal procedures he performed

- Intuitive understanding of mechanical and electronic systems—can "feel" how tech works
- Perfect spatial reasoning allowing mental 3D modeling
- Exceptional hand-eye coordination for micro-scale work

### Trained Skills

**Professional:**
- Advanced cybernetic engineering (Master level) - self-taught and practiced
- Neural interface design (Expert level) - learned through necessity
- System architecture (Expert level) - salvaged manuals and experimentation
- Improvised repair techniques (Master level) - years of underground work
- Augmentation surgery (Advanced level) - learned through desperation and practice

**Personal:**
- Electronics repair and building (Master level)
- Hardware hacking (Expert level)
- Software development (Expert level)
- Reading pre-collapse technical documentation

**Hidden/Secret:**
- Weaponizing everyday tech - doesn't advertise this ability
- Black market contact networks - keeps separate from Phoenix Recovery

- **Fighting Style:** Avoidance first; technical solutions (EMP, system shutdowns)

- **Tactical Thinking:** Problem-solving focused rather than combat strategy

### Overall Competency Ratings
- **Intelligence:** 9/10 (genius-level technical)
- **Physical Strength:** 3/10 (small frame, limited muscle)
- **Agility/Reflexes:** 5/10 (precise hands, but not athletic)

## Writer's Notes

### Writing This Character

**Voice Consistency:**
- Rapid technical speech with engineering metaphors when comfortable
- Awkward and stumbling when discussing emotions
- Always uses precise, specific language even in casual conversation

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- Making him too socially competent—he's genuinely awkward
- Forgetting his obsessive work habits—he doesn't maintain work-life balance
- Having him sleep/eat normally—he neglects basic needs
- Making him physically capable in fights—he's fragile

**Character Strengths in Scenes:**
- Technical problem-solving scenes showcase his genius
- Moments of vulnerability reveal his humanity
- Interactions with Kyr4n show his growth and devotion
