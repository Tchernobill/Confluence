# Border March

> *"Paranoid defenders of the frontier—they stand between the Shore and the void, watching for threats from the Citadel and the wastelands beyond."*

---

## Basic Information

### Identity
- **Official Name:** Border March
- **Common Names/Nicknames:** The March, The Frontier, Border Guards
- **Faction Type:** Militarized frontier territory / Defensive buffer
- **Founded:** ~2090 (post-Renewal defensive organization)
- **Status:** Active, maintains critical defensive role
- **Estimated Population:** ~250,000

### Territory & Influence
- **Primary Territory:** Outskirts along autoroutes and wasteland buffer zones
- **Secondary Areas:** Watchtowers, outposts, checkpoints
- **Territorial Borders:** 
  - Forms outer ring around all other territories
  - North: Citadel threat
  - South/East/West: Wastelands and raiders
  - Inner: All other Shore territories benefit from their defense
- **Key Locations:** 
  - Châteauguay Border Stack (15 units high, defensive positions, ~150,000 population)
  - Border Outposts (watchtowers and control points)
  - Castle-way/Châteauguay (smuggling route hub, ironic given defensive role)
  - North Banks (north shore access points, abandoned but monitored)
  - Ice Harbor (winter facilities, seasonal operations)
  - Security Towers (overlooking roadways and approaches)
- **Sphere of Influence:** Frontier defense, threat monitoring, early warning, controlled access

## Values, Culture & Goals

### Core Values
- **Vigilant Defense:** Constant watchfulness for external threats
- **Paranoid Security:** Trust no outsiders, verify everything
- **Service to Shore:** Protect all territories from external dangers
- **Self-Reliance:** Frontier survival mentality
- **Sacrifice:** Accept danger to shield others

### Cultural Identity
- **Language/Dialect:** Franglais Patois with military terminology
- **Customs & Traditions:** 
  - Watchtower rotations and protocols
  - Threat assessment briefings
  - Frontier survival skills
  - Military discipline and hierarchy
  - Patrol traditions
- **Internal Culture:** 
  - Militarized lifestyle
  - Paranoia about external threats
  - Focus on Citadel (north) and wasteland raiders (south/east/west)
  - Order vs. chaos mentality
  - Frontier hardiness
  - Service identity (protecting others)
- **Public Perception:** 
  - Internally: Essential defenders, underappreciated
  - Externally: Paranoid but necessary, respect for service
  - Reputation: Tough, reliable, suspicious

### Primary Goals
1. **Short-term:** Maintain defensive perimeter, monitor threats
2. **Medium-term:** Improve defensive capabilities, early warning systems
3. **Long-term:** Permanent security for Shore against external threats
4. **Ultimate Vision:** Stable frontier protecting Shore civilization
