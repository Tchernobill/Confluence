## Scene Information

**Summary**: After a profitable market day, Kyr4n ventures into unclaimed territory in the abandoned industrial district at sunset. He discovers a massive brutalist waste processing building and explores it in a deeply meditative state, reading its pipes and systems like blueprints while descending through multiple levels into complete darkness. Deep in the basement, his flashlight catches a reflection—a high-quality android head in pristine condition. Recognizing its sophisticated construction and potential, he carefully wraps it and returns home as stars emerge, his mind already engaged with this new puzzle that represents more than mere salvage.

**POV Character(s)**: Kyr4n (third person limited, deeply internal perspective throughout silent scene)

**Timeline**: 2108-05-01, 17:00-20:30 (approximately 3.5 hours). Same day as scenes 1.01.001-1.01.003. Evening of his first full day shown in the novel. Kyr4n is 18 years old, approximately 6 months after mother's death.

**Location(s)**:

- Regional: South Shore Slum area
- District: Abandoned Industrial District (unclaimed territory)
- Specific: Massive waste processing/incinerator building - brutalist architecture, multiple levels descending to vast basement with concrete pillars

**Key Events**:
