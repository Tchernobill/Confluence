## üé≠ Scene Dynamics

### Point of View Notes
**POV Character:** Kyr4n (third-person limited)
**POV Limitations:** Cannot directly know Riley and Isa's private thoughts or coordination beforehand; interprets social cues through lens of hypervigilance and isolation
**Bias/Perspective:** Sees normalcy as alien/threatening; analyzes social interactions like technical problems; underestimates own worthiness of care
**Internal Conflict:** Desire for connection vs fear of vulnerability; craving normalcy vs not knowing how to achieve it; attraction to both vs not understanding polyamory

### Character Objectives & Obstacles
**Kyr4n:**
- Want: To get through dinner without embarrassing himself or retreating
- Need: To accept care and connection; to believe he deserves better than his hideout
- Obstacle: Years of isolation conditioning; fear of losing control; not understanding social protocols
- Tactics: Over-analyzing everything; deflecting with technical talk; initially maintaining physical distance

**Isa:**
- Want: To make Kyr4n comfortable enough to stay; to integrate Riley naturally
- Need: To build the family structure she's always craved
- Obstacle: Kyr4n's resistance; balancing both men's needs; not pushing too hard
- Tactics: Physical touch for grounding; direct but gentle communication; creating easy domestic atmosphere

**Riley:**
- Want: To support both of them without imposing; to find his place in their dynamic
- Need: To be included in something meaningful; to care for people who value him
- Obstacle: Uncertainty about his role; not wanting to intrude; attraction to both without clear permission
- Tactics: Being useful (cooking); gentle humor; patient observation; respecting boundaries

### Relationship Dynamics in This Scene
- **Kyr4n/Isa:** She anchors him with touch and directness; he trusts her enough to stay despite fear; physical comfort established
- **Kyr4n/Riley:** First extended time together; Kyr4n opens up about Sedh; mutual attraction acknowledged through proximity
- **Isa/Riley:** Easy coordination; both working to make Kyr4n comfortable; flirtation through domestic partnership
- **Power Balance:** Isa has most power (her space, her invitation) but uses it to empower both men; shifts to collaborative by end
- **Subtext:** All three attracted to each other but only Isa fully acknowledges it; Kyr4n processing in real-time; Riley waiting for permission
