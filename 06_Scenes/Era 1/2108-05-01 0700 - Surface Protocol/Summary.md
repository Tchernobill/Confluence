## Scene Information

**Summary**: Kyr4n executes his daily journey from his hidden underground shelter to the underground market, navigating through flooded maintenance tunnels and gang-controlled slum streets. The journey demonstrates his extreme security protocols, intimate knowledge of urban infrastructure, physical capabilities, and the harsh dystopian environment he must navigate to survive. His methodical approach reveals both competence and deep paranoia developed through necessity.

**POV Character(s)**: Kyr4n (close third-person limited throughout)
