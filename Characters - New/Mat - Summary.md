## Quick Reference

- **Role:** Information Broker / Data Archaeologist / Technical Specialist
- **One-Line Description:** Mysteriously aloof data archaeologist who treats information like literature and people like corrupted files
- **Current Arc:** Arc 2+ (Recurring specialist contact)
- **Current Status:** Independent contractor with occasional Phoenix Recovery collaboration

---

## Physical Appearance

- **Distinctive Features:** Electric blue hair (changes frequently), small scar through left eyebrow, geometric tattoos on forearms that function as data storage
- **Typical Clothing:** Vintage band t-shirts, worn jeans, rotating collection of jackets and hoodies, always wears fingerless gloves
- **Body Language:** Lean and wiry build, moves with casual grace, often distant or calculating expression

---

## Core Personality

- **Primary Trait:** Mysteriously aloof with sardonic wit and casual brilliance
- **Secondary Trait:** Emotionally guarded but becomes genuinely enthusiastic about information puzzles
- **Fatal Flaw:** Commitment issues and tendency to disappear when relationships get complicated
- **Core Motivation:** Uncovering the "big truth" behind corporate information control while maintaining absolute freedom

---

## Speech Patterns

- **Speaking Style:** Dry, sardonic tone with deadpan delivery, uses technical metaphors casually
- **Distinctive Phrases:** Quotes obscure data fragments, speaks in blunt technical assessments
- **Accent/Dialect:** Corporate Standard with occasional technical cant
- **Sample Dialogue:**
    - Casual: "Information wants to be free, but everyone has secrets worth keeping."
    - Angry: "Clean data extraction, minimal processing time. Catastrophic neural feedback."
    - Stressed: "Actually, I do have a better idea. Neural cascade override."

---

## Key Mannerisms

- **When Nervous:** Changes appearance more frequently, checks multiple exit strategies
- **When Happy:** Rare bursts of genuine enthusiasm about data patterns and information systems
- **When Thinking:** Flexes fingerless gloves, examines technical displays with clinical interest
- **Signature Habit:** Always has three different exit strategies, quotes deleted corporate emails like literature

---

## Current State

- **Emotional Condition:** Professionally detached but secretly fascinated by Kyr4n's organization's data patterns
- **Primary Goal:** Building comprehensive map of corporate information control networks
- **Biggest Fear:** Being trapped, controlled, or truly known by others
- **Key Relationships:** Kyr4n's organization (professional respect), former mentor (disappeared), corporate family (distant)

---

## Writer's Notes

- **Voice Reminder:** Sardonic and blunt, treats people like data problems, becomes animated about information
- **Consistency Check:** Always maintains emotional distance, never fully commits to situations
- **Scene Strengths:** Technical problem-solving, information revelation, morally complex decision points
