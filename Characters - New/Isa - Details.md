## Basic Information

- **Full Name:** Isa Mercer
- **Aliases/Nicknames:** "Wire" (underground dealer handle), "M" (close friends)
- **Age:** Born 2091 (17 years old in Arc 1)
- **Gender/Pronouns:** Female, She/Her
- **Birthplace:** South Shore Medical District (before it became a slum)
- **Current Residence:** South Shore Slum
- **Occupation:** Underground implant dealer transitioning to Business Operations Manager at Phoenix Recovery
- **Social Status:** Underground economy mid-tier rising to emerging legitimate entrepreneur
- **Languages:** Corporate Standard (fluent), Street Cant (native), Basic Mandarin, Tech Shorthand
- **Religion/Beliefs:** Practical humanism, believes in earned loyalty and second chances

---

## Physical Description

- **Height/Build:** 5'5" (165cm), medium build with hidden strength, dancer-like grace
- **Hair:** Dark brown with subtle red highlights, shoulder-length, often tucked behind ears
- **Eyes:** Hazel with gold flecks that become prominent when excited
- **Skin:** Pale from limited sunlight
- **Scars/Marks:** Lower back tattoo of a snake, bumpy mole on hip, small scar on left shoulder (shrapnel from corporate raid), intricate circuit-pattern UV tattoo behind right ear (underground dealer mark)
- **Clothing Style:** Practical street fashion - reinforced leather jacket, fitted pants with hidden pockets, sturdy boots, minimal jewelry
- **Accessories:** Basic health monitor implant, encrypted comm unit (subdermal), enhanced reflexes (minor muscle twitch accelerators)
- **Health Status:** Excellent physical condition, minor anxiety responses to corporate security
- **Physical Limitations:** None significant

## Psychology & Personality

- **Core Values:** Actions define people more than origins, loyalty earned through consistency, everyone deserves second chances
- **Moral Philosophy:** Morally flexible but fundamentally good, believes in practical solutions over rigid principles
- **Temperament:** Emotionally intelligent, pragmatically optimistic, quietly confident, socially adaptable
- **Intelligence Type:** High emotional intelligence, excellent pattern recognition in human behavior
- **Learning Style:** Interpersonal learning, learns by watching and interacting
- **Coping Mechanisms:** Uses humor to defuse tension, builds relationships for emotional security
- **Stress Responses:** Becomes more protective of relationships, increased vigilance for betrayal signs
- **Humor Style:** Warm, inclusive, never at someone's expense
- **Risk Tolerance:** Calculated risks for relationship building and business growth
- **Authority Relationship:** Naturally skeptical of formal authority, prefers earned respect

- **Family Background:**
    - Parents: Deceased (killed in corporate "pacification" operation)

    - Extended Family: None known
    - Family Dynamics: Effectively orphaned, learned self-reliance early
- **Childhood:** Witnessed parents' death in corporate raid, survived in underground economy, learned people skills for survival
- **Education:** Street education in human nature, underground dealer training, self-taught business basics
- **Career History:** Underground implant dealer → Phoenix Recovery partner
- **Significant Relationships:**
    - Kyr4n: Growing business partnership with romantic attraction
    - Underground dealer network: Professional relationships built on trust
- **Major Life Events:**
    - **Age ~8**: Parents killed in corporate raid, became orphan
    - **Age ~12-16**: Established herself in underground economy
    - **Age 17**: Meets Kyr4n, begins Phoenix Recovery partnership
- **Secrets:** Deeper emotional needs than she shows, fear of vulnerability
- **Regrets:** Not being able to save her parents, some questionable underground deals

## Skills & Abilities

- **Natural Talents:** Emotional intelligence, pattern recognition in human behavior, natural charisma, memory for faces and names
- **Trained Skills:** Advanced negotiation and mediation, underground network navigation, basic electronics, business management
- **Professional Expertise:** Deal-making, trust building, social bridge between different groups
- **Combat Abilities:** Proficient with mono-blade and shock batons, basic firearms, prefers evasion and de-escalation
- **Special Powers:** None - purely human capabilities
- **Languages/Technical Skills:** Basic tech knowledge, excellent at reading people and situations
- **Weaknesses/Limitations:** Fear of abandonment can cloud judgment, sometimes overly protective
- **Areas for Growth:** Learning legitimate business practices, developing deeper technical knowledge
