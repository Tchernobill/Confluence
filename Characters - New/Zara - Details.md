## Physical Description

- **Height/Build:** 5'6" (168cm), compact muscular build, athletic dancer's physique optimized for combat
- **Hair:** Black hair in practical ponytail, occasionally loose for special occasions
- **Eyes:** Intense dark brown, almost black
- **Skin:** Olive complexion with slight tan from outdoor training
- **Scars/Marks:** Knife scar across left ribs (first assassination attempt), burn mark on right shoulder blade (chemical interrogation), small tattoo of a flame behind right ear (personal symbol), callused knuckles and palms from weapons training
- **Clothing Style:** Form-fitting tactical gear, concealed weapons, practical boots, minimal accessories
- **Accessories:** Basic health monitor implant, enhanced reflexes (muscle accelerators), encrypted communication suite
- **Health Status:** Peak physical condition, enhanced reflexes from augmentation
- **Physical Limitations:** Trauma responses can affect sleep patterns and hypervigilance

## Psychology & Personality

- **Core Values:** Strength protects what matters, weakness invites exploitation, loyalty must be absolute or worthless
- **Moral Philosophy:** Authority comes from ability to protect or destroy, society is predator/prey dynamics with better marketing
- **Temperament:** Fierce, protective, direct, fearless, intensely loyal, violence-focused problem solver
- **Intelligence Type:** Tactical and situational intelligence, exceptional at reading threats and body language
- **Learning Style:** Physical practice, trial under pressure, learning through conflict
- **Coping Mechanisms:** Blade sharpening as meditation, daily combat forms practice, physical exercise
- **Stress Responses:** Increased vigilance, weapon checking, protective positioning
- **Humor Style:** Dark, sarcastic, often involving violence metaphors
- **Risk Tolerance:** High for personal safety, zero tolerance for threats to protected individuals
- **Authority Relationship:** Respects only those who demonstrate superior strength or earn absolute trust
