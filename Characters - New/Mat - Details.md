# Detailed Character Sheet: Mat "Wire" Chang

## Basic Information

- **Full Name:** Mat Chang
- **Aliases/Nicknames:** "Wire" (professional handle), "Riley" (some sources reference this name)
- **Age:** 29 years old
- **Gender/Pronouns:** Male, He/Him
- **Birthplace:** Neo-Montreal Corporate Zone, mid-tier corporate housing district
- **Current Residence:** Converted server room in abandoned office building
- **Occupation:** Data Archaeologist / Information Broker
- **Social Status:** Underground economy elite (makes good money but lives off-grid)
- **Languages:** Corporate Standard, Korean, Data Query languages, Street Cant, Corporate Legal
- **Religion/Beliefs:** Information wants to be free, but everyone has secrets worth keeping

---

## Physical Description

- **Height/Build:** 5'8" (173cm), lean and wiry, moves with casual grace
- **Hair:** Changes frequently - currently electric blue, previously black, green, silver. Always styled differently, often asymmetrical
- **Eyes:** Dark brown, often distant or calculating
- **Skin:** Light olive, slightly pale from spending time indoors
- **Scars/Marks:** Small scar through left eyebrow, subtle geometric tattoos on forearms that function as data storage, callused fingertips from constant typing
- **Clothing Style:** Vintage band t-shirts, worn jeans, collection of different jackets and hoodies that he cycles through
- **Accessories:** Always wears fingerless gloves, neural interface jack (hidden under hair)
- **Health Status:** Good physical condition despite indoor lifestyle
- **Physical Limitations:** Minimal combat training, prefers to avoid physical confrontation

---

## Psychology & Personality

- **Core Values:** Information is power but should be distributed, secrets are fine but lies are not
- **Moral Philosophy:** Most social structures are elaborate information control schemes
- **Temperament:** Mysteriously aloof, sardonic wit, emotionally guarded, fiercely independent, casually brilliant
- **Intelligence Type:** Exceptional pattern recognition, intuitive understanding of information systems
- **Learning Style:** Self-taught through experimentation, learns by diving deep into data patterns
- **Coping Mechanisms:** Changes appearance when stressed, maintains multiple exit strategies, emotional detachment
- **Stress Responses:** Becomes more aloof, increases security measures, considers disappearing entirely
- **Humor Style:** Dry, sardonic, often involves technical metaphors and data references
- **Risk Tolerance:** High for information gathering, low for personal emotional investment
- **Authority Relationship:** Deeply suspicious of any authority, sees most power structures as information control

---

## Background & History

- **Family Background:**
    - Parents: Work in corporate data management, relationship is distant and formal
    - Siblings: Younger sister who's a corporate lawyer (they don't talk)
    - Extended Family: Generally involved in corporate systems he opposes
    - Family Dynamics: Corporate family that prioritized work over relationships, creating abandonment issues
- **Childhood:** Raised in sterile corporate environment, discovered hacking as escape from family expectations
- **Education:** Corporate technical school (dropped out), self-taught advanced techniques
- **Career History:** Corporate track → Underground data archaeology → Independent information broker
- **Significant Relationships:**
    - Former mentor: Underground data archaeologist who was "disappeared" by corporate security
    - Series of casual relationships that ended when things got "too real"
    - Ongoing rivalry with corporate data security teams
- **Major Life Events:**
    - **Age 19**: Discovery of major corporate cover-up
    - **Age 23**: Mentor's disappearance, decision to go fully underground
    - **Age 25**: First major data score that established his reputation
    - **Recent**: Finding evidence of his own family's involvement in data manipulation schemes
- **Secrets:** Full extent of his data on corporate information control, evidence about his family's involvement
- **Regrets:** Not saving important data when his mentor was disappeared, never confronting family about their involvement

---

## Skills & Abilities

- **Natural Talents:** Intuitive understanding of information systems, exceptional memory for data patterns, natural talent for reading people's digital habits
- **Trained Skills:** Advanced hacking techniques, data archaeology, social engineering, counter-surveillance, information verification
- **Professional Expertise:** Elite-level data manipulation, expert in multiple programming languages, specialized in deleted data recovery
- **Combat Abilities:** Minimal combat training, good at running and hiding, modified stun pistol (rarely carried)
- **Special Powers:** None - purely human technological expertise
- **Languages/Technical Skills:** Expert in multiple programming languages, specialized in quantum decryption, data pattern analysis
- **Weaknesses/Limitations:** Physical vulnerability, gets obsessed with data puzzles and ignores practical concerns, social awkwardness in non-digital contexts
- **Areas for Growth:** Learning to trust others, developing genuine emotional connections, balancing information sharing with security

---

## Resources & Possessions

- **Financial Status:** Significant digital currency holdings, multiple fake identities
- **Property/Assets:** Various safe houses and data caches, converted server room residence
- **Important Possessions:** Photo of first mentor, collection of "ghost data" from deleted corporate files, vintage music player
- **Weapons/Tools:** Custom data mining rigs, portable quantum decryption device, collection of vintage and cutting-edge storage media
- **Transportation:** Modified motorcycle with stealth capabilities, prefers public transport to avoid tracking
- **Contacts/Network:** Network of information brokers, some underground journalists, former corporate employees with access
- **Debts/Obligations:** Various information trades, ongoing rivalry with corporate security
