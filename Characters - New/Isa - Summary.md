## Quick Reference

- **Role:** Arc Protagonist / Business Partner
- **One-Line Description:** Emotionally intelligent underground dealer who becomes legitimate business catalyst and emotional anchor for isolated genius
- **Current Arc:** Arc 1 - The Scavenger
- **Current Status:** Building Phoenix Recovery partnership with Kyr4n, transitioning from underground to legitimate business

---

## Physical Appearance

- **Distinctive Features:** Hazel eyes with gold flecks that shine when excited, dark brown hair with red highlights tucked behind ears
- **Typical Clothing:** Practical street fashion - reinforced leather jacket, fitted pants with hidden pockets, sturdy boots
- **Body Language:** Dancer-like grace, unconsciously mirrors others' posture, touches people casually when comfortable

---

## Core Personality

- **Primary Trait:** Emotionally intelligent with natural ability to read and connect with people
- **Secondary Trait:** Pragmatically optimistic - sees potential for improvement in broken situations
- **Fatal Flaw:** Fear of abandonment and deep commitment despite craving both
- **Core Motivation:** Building something meaningful beyond survival, creating the family she never had

---

## Speech Patterns

- **Speaking Style:** Firm but casual with strangers, warm and velvety when alone with Kyr4n
- **Distinctive Phrases:** Often asks clarifying questions, uses humor to defuse tension
- **Accent/Dialect:** Slight street accent that softens around Kyr4n
- **Sample Dialogue:**
    - Casual: "Look, we both know this deal could work, but let's be honest about the risks."
    - Angry: "Don't you dare shut me out when I'm trying to help you."
    - Stressed: "I need to understand what's happening here - help me help you."

---

## Key Mannerisms

- **When Nervous:** Unconsciously mirrors other people's posture more intensely
- **When Happy:** High-pitched hyena-like laugh, gold flecks in eyes become prominent
- **When Thinking:** Hums while working with her hands, raises eyebrow when skeptical
- **Signature Habit:** Touches people while talking when comfortable, never afraid to speak her mind

---

## Current State

- **Emotional Condition:** Hopeful but cautious, sensing Kyr4n is hiding something important
- **Primary Goal:** Establish Phoenix Recovery as legitimate business while deepening relationship with Kyr4n
- **Biggest Fear:** Being abandoned just as she begins to trust and invest emotionally
- **Key Relationships:** Kyr4n (growing partnership/attraction), underground dealer network, emerging legitimate contacts

---

## Writer's Notes

- **Voice Reminder:** Warm, direct, asks probing questions, uses casual touch and humor to connect
- **Consistency Check:** Always balances business pragmatism with emotional intelligence
- **Scene Strengths:** Negotiation scenes, emotional catalyst moments, social bridge situations
