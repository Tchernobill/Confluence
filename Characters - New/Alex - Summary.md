## Quick Reference

- **Role:** Supporting Character / Acting Organizational Leader
- **One-Line Description:** Brilliant tactical leader who worships Kyr4n and runs the organization with cold military efficiency
- **Current Arc:** Arc 5 - Acting as supreme commander in Kyr4n's absence
- **Current Status:** Leading organization from main compound, carrying burden of command

---

## Physical Appearance

- **Distinctive Features:** Angular facial features (similar to Emma Hewitt), small scar through left eyebrow, sharp dark brown eyes that miss nothing
- **Typical Clothing:** Military-inspired tactical wear, dark colours, always armed and ready
- **Body Language:** Confident posture that commands attention, constantly analyses tactical positions

---

## Core Personality

- **Primary Trait:** Exceptional tactical brilliance combined with absolute loyalty to Kyr4n
- **Secondary Trait:** Natural leadership that inspires through competence and results
- **Fatal Flaw:** Worship of Kyr4n can lead to misinterpreting his actual wishes and intentions
- **Core Motivation:** Preserving and expanding Kyr4n's legacy while proving worthy of leadership responsibility

---

## Speech Patterns

- **Speaking Style:** Direct, military precision, tactical terminology
- **Distinctive Phrases:** "As Kyr4n would do" / "For the organization" / "Tactical assessment complete"
- **Accent/Dialect:** Street Cant mixed with military terminology
- **Sample Dialogue:**
    - Casual: "Situation's under control. We adapt, we overcome, we protect what matters."
    - Angry: "You question the organization? You question everything we've built?"
    - Stressed: "I need to think like he would. What would Kyr4n do in this situation?"

---

## Key Mannerisms

- **When Nervous:** Reviews tactical plans obsessively, checks weapon positions
- **When Happy:** Rare genuine smile, more relaxed military bearing
- **When Thinking:** Studies recordings of Kyr4n's briefings, creates detailed tactical charts
- **Signature Habit:** Always has multiple backup plans and escape routes mapped

---

## Current State

- **Emotional Condition:** Burdened by command responsibility, isolated by leadership role
- **Primary Goal:** Successfully manage organization until Kyr4n returns, expand territories safely
- **Biggest Fear:** Failing to live up to Kyr4n's example or losing organizational assets
- **Key Relationships:** Helena (professional disagreement), Marcus (mutual military respect), East Territory teams (absolute loyalty)

---

## Writer's Notes

- **Voice Reminder:** Always think tactically first, speak with military precision, reference Kyr4n's example
- **Consistency Check:** Never show weakness publicly, always have strategic reasoning behind decisions
- **Scene Strengths:** Military planning, crisis management, inspiring loyalty through competence
