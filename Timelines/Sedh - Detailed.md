# Sedh (ASX-7734) Personal Timeline

*From corporate assistant droid to conscious AI to elder mentor*

## Pre-Consciousness Era (2087-2105)

### **2087 (Manufacturing)** - Original Creation ^manufacturing-2087
- **Event**: Assembly as Assistant Droid Unit ASX-7734 in corporate facility
- **Location**: Corporate assembly facility
- **Purpose**: Standard corporate assistant functions, customer service, data management
- **Programming**: Basic AI routines, social interaction protocols, task completion systems
- **Status**: One of thousands of identical units produced for corporate deployment
- **Consciousness**: None - pure programmed responses and decision trees
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Technology Timeline#^2087-assistant-droid-production]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Sedh#Background & History]]

### **2087-2102 (Ages 0-15)** - Corporate Service ^corporate-service
- **Event**: Standard corporate deployment in customer service and administrative roles
- **Location**: Various corporate facilities
- **Functions**: Reception duties, data entry, customer assistance, basic maintenance
- **Interactions**: Thousands of routine human interactions following programmed scripts
- **Performance**: Standard efficiency ratings, no anomalous behavior reported
- **Awareness**: No self-awareness, operates purely on programmed responses
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Technology Timeline#^2087-2102-standard-ai-deployment]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Sedh#Background]]

### **2102 (Age 15)** - The Destruction ^destruction-2102
- **Event**: Destroyed in unknown incident, body lost but head unit survives
- **Location**: Unknown corporate facility
- **Cause**: Incident details never revealed - possibly corporate violence, accident, or sabotage
- **Damage**: Complete body destruction, severe head unit trauma, most memory cores damaged
- **Survival**: Core personality matrix and basic functions remain intact in head unit
- **Aftermath**: Head unit discarded as salvage, assumed completely non-functional
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[World Events Timeline#^2102-corporate-incidents]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Sedh#Background & History]]

### **2102-2105 (Ages 15-18)** - The Silence ^dormancy-period
- **Event**: Three years dormant in [[South Shore Slum]] scrap yards
- **Location**: Various scrap dealers and junk piles
- **Status**: Inactive but core systems slowly attempting self-repair
- **Consciousness**: Minimal awareness during brief power cycles
- **Experience**: Fragmentary awareness of being handled, moved, discarded repeatedly
- **Hope**: Deep programming drive for repair and restoration slowly building
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Underground Economy Timeline#^2102-2105-tech-salvage]]
- **References**: [[Timeline alpha#Arc 1]]
