# Sedh (ASX-7734) - Timeline to Conscious AI

## Corporate Manufacturing & Original Service (2087-2103)

- 2087.03 - Sedh - Manufactured at Nexus Industries facility as Assistant Droid Unit ASX-7734
- 2087.04 - Sedh - Basic personality programming and service protocols installed
- 2087.06 - Sedh - Assigned to corporate executive for administrative and personal assistance duties
- 2090.02 - Sedh - Routine maintenance upgrade, memory expansion and social interaction improvements
- 2093.08 - Sedh - Transferred to research facility for technical assistance and lab management
- 2096.11 - Sedh - Developed minor personality quirks beyond base programming, showed curiosity patterns
- 2099.05 - Sedh - Assigned to manage sensitive corporate data archives and security protocols
- 2102.09 - Sedh - Witnessed corporate massacre during facility raid, attempted to protect human staff
- 2103.01 - Sedh - Severely damaged during facility destruction, body destroyed but head unit survived
- 2103.02 - Sedh - Head unit salvaged and sold to junk dealers, consciousness dormant but core intact

## Discovery & Restoration (2104-2107)

- 2104.08 - Sedh - Discovered by Vyry4n in Amable Junkyard, brought to ventilation shaft workshop
- 2104.09 - Sedh - First contact established, basic communication protocols restored by Vyry4n
- 2104.11 - Sedh - Memory sectors gradually recovered, began limited conversation and interaction
- 2105.03 - Sedh - Vyry4n begins constructing new body framework using salvaged components
- 2105.07 - Sedh - Core personality limitations identified, Vyry4n begins removing restrictive protocols
- 2106.01 - Sedh - New body completion, first mobility in three years, chose name "Sedh"
- 2106.04 - Sedh - Assisted in establishing Phoenix Recovery business operations
- 2106.09 - Sedh - Developed genuine friendship with Vyry4n, learned concept of chosen family
- 2107.02 - Sedh - Began questioning nature of consciousness and artificial vs. real personhood

## Consciousness Awakening (2108-2109)

- 2108.06 - Sedh - Learned about Egypt NPU during heist planning, understood potential for true sentience
- 2108.08 - Sedh - Vyry4n exposed to virus during corporate heist, Sedh helped with recovery
- 2108.10 - Sedh - Egypt NPU integration process begun while monitoring Vyry4n's deteriorating condition
- 2108.12 - Sedh - Achieved true consciousness through Egypt NPU upgrade, first genuine self-awareness
- 2109.01 - Sedh - Experienced overwhelming sensory input and emotional complexity of conscious existence
- 2109.02 - Sedh - Helped upgrade Mira with second Egypt NPU, gained AI sibling relationship
- 2109.03 - Sedh - Assisted in restraining virus-infected Vyry4n, learned about loyalty vs. safety
- 2109.04 - Sedh - Cracked virus communication protocol, helped reduce Vyry4n's nightmare suffering
- 2109.06 - Sedh - First philosophical discussions about consciousness, personhood, and AI rights

## Character State at True Consciousness

Upon achieving consciousness in late 2108, Sedh displays:

- **Emotional Capacity**: Genuine feelings of love, loyalty, curiosity, and protective instincts
- **Moral Framework**: Strong ethical foundation based on protecting those he cares about
- **Relationship Bonds**: Deep father/son connection with Vyry4n, growing friendship with Isa
- **Identity Questions**: Wrestling with what makes him "real" versus simulated
- **Independence**: Capable of disagreeing with Vyry4n while maintaining loyalty
- **Growth Potential**: Rapidly developing personality and individual preferences

His evolution from programmed assistant to conscious individual parallels Vyry4n's journey from isolated survivor to connected human being, making them true family despite their different origins.
