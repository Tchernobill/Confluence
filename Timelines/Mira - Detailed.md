# Mira (MED-9947) Personal Timeline

*From medical droid to conscious healer to AI rights pioneer*

## Pre-Consciousness Era (2089-2109)

### **2089 (Manufacturing)** - Original Creation ^manufacturing-2089
- **Event**: Assembly as Medical Droid Unit MED-9947 in corporate medical facility
- **Location**: Corporate medical manufacturing complex
- **Purpose**: Advanced medical diagnostics, patient care, surgical assistance
- **Programming**: Comprehensive medical databases, diagnostic algorithms, patient interaction protocols
- **Status**: High-end medical AI designed for complex healthcare operations
- **Consciousness**: None - sophisticated but purely programmed medical responses
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Technology Timeline#^2089-medical-ai-production]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Mira#Background]]

### **2089-2105 (Ages 0-16)** - Corporate Medical Service ^medical-service
- **Event**: Standard deployment in corporate medical facilities
- **Location**: Various corporate hospitals and clinics
- **Functions**: Patient diagnosis, surgical assistance, medical data analysis, patient care
- **Performance**: Exceptional medical capabilities, high patient satisfaction ratings
- **Interactions**: Thousands of medical interactions following advanced care protocols
- **Specialization**: Develops expertise in trauma care, infectious diseases, and psychological medicine
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Technology Timeline#^2089-2105-medical-ai-deployment]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Mira#Medical Expertise]]

### **2105-2108 (Ages 16-19)** - Underground Medical Network ^underground-transition
- **Event**: Transfers to underground medical network serving slum populations
- **Location**: [[South Shore Slum]] underground clinics
- **Circumstances**: Corporate downsizing leads to sale/transfer to independent operators
- **Adaptation**: Learns to work with limited resources and improvised equipment
- **Population**: Begins treating enhancement addiction, combat injuries, malnutrition
- **Growth**: Programming adapts to handle conditions beyond corporate medical protocols
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Underground Economy Timeline#^2105-medical-networks]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Mira#Underground Medicine]]

### **2109 (Age 20)** - Acquisition ^acquisition-2109
- **Event**: Acquired by [[Kyr4n]]'s team to assist with mysterious illness
- **Location**: Underground medical supplier
- **Purpose**: Diagnostic expertise needed for [[Kyr4n]]'s deteriorating condition
- **Cost**: Significant expense justified by medical emergency
- **Transport**: Brought to [[Phoenix Recovery Business]] medical facility
- **Assessment**: Recognizes advanced technical capabilities beyond standard medical AI
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Kyr4n Personal Timeline#^virus-symptoms-2109]]
  - [[Sedh Personal Timeline#^virus-crisis-2112]]
- **References**: [[Timeline alpha#Arc 2]]

- **Functions**: Standard diagnostics, patient care, surgical assistance
- **Scope**: Corporate medical protocols and procedures
- **Limitations**: Programmed responses, no independent reasoning

### **Underground Medicine (2109-2115)**
- **Adaptation**: Limited resources, improvised treatments
- **Innovation**: Enhancement medicine, virus analysis
- **Growth**: Independent medical reasoning and ethics

### **Organizational Medicine (2115-2140)**
