# Isa Mercer Personal Timeline

*From orphaned survivor to underground entrepreneur to mature partner*

## Early Life (2091-2109)

### **2091 (Birth)** - Beginning ^birth-2091
- **Event**: Born in [[Lore/Geography#South Shore Medical District]] when still respectable
- **Location**: Former South Shore Medical District
- **Family**: Elena Delacroix (nurse), Daniel Mercer (maintenance tech)
- **Context**: Born before area's transformation into slum
- **Heritage**: Mixed European/Asian descent, third-generation slum dweller
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[World Events Timeline#^2091-medical-district-decline]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Isa#Background]]

### **2091-2103 (Ages 0-12)** - Childhood ^childhood-era
- **Event**: Grows up during South Shore's decline from respectable to dangerous
- **Location**: Deteriorating [[Lore/Geography#South Shore Medical District]]
- **Family**: Stable working-class family with medical connections
- **Education**: Basic schooling before infrastructure collapse
- **Personality**: Develops adaptability and social intelligence
- **Skills**: Early exposure to medical technology through mother's work
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[World Events Timeline#^2091-2103-district-transformation]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Isa#Upbringing]]

### **2103 (Age 12)** - The Loss ^parents-death-2103
- **Event**: Parents killed during corporate "urban renewal" operation
- **Location**: [[Lore/Geography#South Shore Medical District]]
- **Trauma**: Witnesses corporate violence, forced into survival mode
- **Aftermath**: Becomes street orphan, learns underground survival
- **Impact**: Foundation of distrust toward corporate authority
- **Survival**: Taken in by underground dealer networks
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[World Events Timeline#^2103-corporate-pacification]]
  - [[Vyry4n Personal Timeline#^2103-slum-riots]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Isa#Defining Moments]]

### **2103-2106 (Ages 12-15)** - Street Education ^street-survival
- **Event**: Learns underground economy and survival skills
- **Location**: [[Lore/Geography#South Shore Slum]] networks
- **Mentors**: Tommy "Splice" Martinez, Dr. Sarah Kim (underground medic)
- **Skills**: Basic electronics, negotiation, social manipulation, first aid
- **Reputation**: Begins building network of contacts and trust
- **Business**: First successful deals establish her reliability
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Underground Economy Timeline#^2103-youth-networks]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Isa#Education & Training]]

### **2107-2109 (Ages 16-18)** - Underground Entrepreneur ^early-business
- **Event**: Establishes herself as independent implant dealer
- **Location**: [[Underground Tech Market]]s throughout [[South Shore Slum]]
- **Specialty**: Quality implants for clients who value discretion
- **Network**: Builds extensive contact system with dealers and clients
- **Reputation**: Known for fairness and reliability in dangerous trade
- **Skills**: Advanced negotiation, medical device assessment, risk management
- **Related Events**: 
  - [[Technology Timeline#^2107-underground-implant-trade]]
- **References**: [[Characters/Isa#Occupation]]

## Character Development Arcs

### **Survivor to Entrepreneur (Ages 12-23)**
- **Trauma**: Parents' death forces rapid maturation
- **Adaptation**: Learns underground economy and survival skills
- **Success**: Builds respected business through competence and ethics
- **Partnership**: Creates stable relationship despite emotional barriers

### **Independence and Growth (Ages 24-46)**
- **Loss**: Virus separation destroys relationship and business
- **Resilience**: Rebuilds life independently with greater sophistication
- **Success**: Achieves significant wealth and status
- **Investigation**: Researches and plans reunion approach

### **Moral Advocate (Ages 47-64)**
- **Courage**: Confronts powerful partner about ethical concerns
- **Influence**: Helps moderate authoritarian tendencies
- **Truth-telling**: Forces moral reckoning despite personal cost
- **Partnership**: Supports democratic transition

### **Legacy Builder (Ages 65-74)**
- **Creation**: Partners in environmental and educational projects
- **Teaching**: Shares wisdom with future generations
- **Peace**: Achieves complete life satisfaction and partnership
- **Completion**: Dies having built lasting positive change
