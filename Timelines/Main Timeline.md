## Character Presence Summary

**Core Characters (Present Throughout):**

- **Vyr4n**: Central protagonist in every scene
- **Sedh**: AI companion from Arc 1.04 onward
- **Mira**: Medical AI from Arc 2.03 onward

**Major Characters (Intermittent Presence):**

- **Isa**: Primary love interest, leaves in Arc 2, returns in Arc 4-5, permanent from Arc 6
- **Marcus**: Lieutenant from Arc 3.05 onward
- **Mother**: Flashbacks and influence throughout, direct presence only in early timeline

**Arc-Specific Characters:**

- **Frank**: Arc 2 only (heist leader)
- **Rico Vasquez**: Arc 3 only (criminal boss overthrown)
- **Corporate Executives**: Arc 4-5 (business/assassination targets)
- **Democratic Leaders**: Arc 6 (transition partners)

**Supporting Cast:**

- Various lieutenants, lovers, business associates, enemies, and community members appearing as needed for specific scenes and character development moments.
