## Street Slang & Local Terms

### Movement & Transportation

- **"Going Crown"** - Attempting to cross to the Citadel
- **"Hitting the Underpass"** - Using the metro smuggling route
- **"Tube-running"** - Traveling through metro tunnels
- **"Bridge-bumping"** - Getting stopped at checkpoints
- **"Ice-running"** - Winter supply missions
- **"River-hopping"** - Crossing water illegally
- **"Deep-diving"** - Going underground
- **"Surface-breaking"** - Coming up from underground hideouts
- **"Line-jumping"** - Using unauthorized metro access
- **"Portal-crashing"** - Forcing entry through JeeCee Portal
- **"Tunnel-ratting"** - Living in underground passages
- **"Grid-dodging"** - Avoiding aerial surveillance
- **"Wall-hugging"** - Staying close to Citadel perimeter undetected

### Technology & Equipment

- **"Crown-tech"** - Advanced Citadel technology
- **"Shore-rigged"** - Jury-rigged, makeshift technology
- **"Grid-watching"** - Monitoring Citadel defenses
- **"Crown-eyes"** - Citadel surveillance drones
- **"Mesh-dancing"** - Hacking into The Mesh network
- **"Signal-bleeding"** - Intercepting Citadel communications
- **"Code-running"** - Data smuggling operations
- **"Wire-walking"** - High-risk hacking
- **"Ghost-boxing"** - Using untraceable communication devices
- **"Heat-sinking"** - Cooling overheated salvaged tech
- **"Power-bleeding"** - Stealing electricity from main grid
- **"Freq-jamming"** - Disrupting surveillance signals
- **"Data-mining"** - Information gathering (different from ore mining)
- **"Bit-running"** - Small-scale data courier work

### Weather & Survival

- **"Heat-chasing"** - Seeking warm shelter
- **"Ice-breaking"** - Surviving harsh winters
- **"Storm-riding"** - Traveling during bad weather for cover
- **"Freeze-time"** - The worst winter months
- **"Thaw-season"** - Brief spring relief
- **"Barrel-warming"** - Gathering around makeshift fires
- **"Pipe-heating"** - Using steam pipes for warmth
- **"Frost-bite weather"** - Extremely dangerous cold
- **"Snow-blind"** - Limited visibility conditions
- **"Ice-locked"** - Trapped by frozen waterways

### Social & Cultural

- **"Shore-side"** - Anything related to the South Shore
- **"Tower-talk"** - Citadel language/accent
- **"Crown-born"** - Someone from the Citadel
- **"Slum-raised"** - Born and raised on the Shore
- **"Wall-crawler"** - Someone who works near the Citadel perimeter
- **"Tunnel-brother/sister"** - Close ally in underground community
- **"Surface-walker"** - Someone who avoids underground areas
- **"Guild-sworn"** - Member of a mining guild
- **"Family-tied"** - Connected to organized crime
- **"Clean-handed"** - Someone who doesn't do dirty work
- **"Blood-credits"** - Favors owed for dangerous work
- **"Shore-bond"** - Deep friendship forged by shared hardship
- **"Crown-kissing"** - Sucking up to Citadel authorities
- **"Tunnel-wisdom"** - Street smarts and survival knowledge

### Mining & Work

- **"Pit-diving"** - Working the deep mines
- **"Ore-bleeding"** - Mining rare earth elements
- **"Rock-breaking"** - General mining work
- **"Dust-lungs"** - Miners with respiratory damage
- **"Shaft-walking"** - Inspecting mine tunnels
- **"Vein-chasing"** - Following ore deposits
- **"Strike-hitting"** - Finding a rich deposit
- **"Cave-in weather"** - Dangerous mining conditions
- **"Shift-pulling"** - Working long mining shifts
- **"Tool-heavy"** - Carrying mining equipment
- **"Guild-marked"** - Officially registered miner
- **"Freelance-digging"** - Unauthorized mining

### Crime & Black Market

- **"Dark-trading"** - Black market transactions
- **"Under-selling"** - Selling stolen goods
- **"Clean-washing"** - Laundering stolen items
- **"Heat-carrying"** - Being wanted by authorities
- **"Ghost-walking"** - Moving undetected
- **"Shadow-dancing"** - Avoiding surveillance
- **"Blood-money"** - Payment for violent work
- **"Dirty-credits"** - Untraceable currency
- **"Fence-jumping"** - Selling to black market dealers
- **"Contract-bleeding"** - Taking assassination jobs
- **"Territory-marking"** - Claiming criminal turf
- **"Protection-buying"** - Paying for safety

### Surveillance & Security

- **"Eye-dodging"** - Avoiding cameras and drones
- **"Sweep-timing"** - Planning around security patrols
- **"Signal-ghosting"** - Moving without electronic trace
- **"Heat-signature dancing"** - Avoiding thermal detection
- **"RF-silence"** - Electronic communication blackout
- **"Observer-burning"** - Destroying surveillance equipment
- **"Grid-static"** - Interference in surveillance network
- **"Watchtower-blind"** - Security surveillance dead zone
- **"Patrol-ghosting"** - Avoiding security rounds
- **"Scanner-dancing"** - Evading detection equipment

### Food & Resources

- **"Real-eating"** - Consuming actual organic food
- **"Synth-choking"** - Eating synthetic/processed food
- **"Water-pure"** - Clean, uncontaminated water
- **"Filter-running"** - Operating water purification
- **"Ration-stretching"** - Making supplies last longer
- **"Garden-raiding"** - Stealing from Citadel hydroponic farms
- **"Barrel-cooking"** - Preparing food over fire barrels
- **"Scrap-hunting"** - Searching for useful materials
- **"Supply-bleeding"** - Intercepting supply shipments

### Violence & Conflict

- **"Tower-rage"** - Anger at the Citadel
- **"Blood-settling"** - Violent revenge
- **"Territory-burning"** - Gang warfare
- **"Authority-bleeding"** - Attacking security forces
- **"Resistance-dancing"** - Participating in organized rebellion
- **"Riot-timing"** - Coordinating civil unrest
- **"Enforcement-dodging"** - Avoiding Corporate Security
- **"Compliance-breaking"** - Violating Citadel laws
- **"Justice-bleeding"** - Vigilante violence

### Status & Reputation

- **"Rep-building"** - Gaining street credibility
- **"Name-carrying"** - Having a feared reputation
- **"Credit-bleeding"** - Being heavily in debt
- **"Trust-banking"** - Building reliable relationships
- **"Word-bond"** - Sacred promise in the underground
- **"Honor-debt"** - Owing someone for past help
- **"Respect-earning"** - Proving yourself worthy
- **"Shame-carrying"** - Being marked as untrustworthy

### Time & Scheduling

- **"Shift-time"** - Work hours (mining/factory)
- **"Dark-hours"** - Night time operations
- **"Grid-down time"** - When surveillance is reduced
- **"Heat-window"** - Brief opportunity for illegal activity
- **"Patrol-gap"** - Time between security sweeps
- **"Storm-window"** - Using bad weather as cover
- **"Maintenance-hours"** - When systems are offline
- **"Sleep-shift"** - Overnight period
- **"Rush-time"** - Peak activity periods

### Emotional/Psychological States

- **"Crown-sick"** - Desperate to reach the Citadel
- **"Shore-mad"** - Mental breakdown from slum conditions
- **"Tunnel-crazy"** - Claustrophobic from underground living
- **"Grid-paranoid"** - Obsessive fear of surveillance
- **"Winter-broken"** - Psychological damage from harsh conditions
- **"Heat-drunk"** - Euphoric from finding warmth
- **"Hope-bleeding"** - Gradually losing optimism
- **"Rage-burning"** - Sustained anger at the system
- **"Dream-chasing"** - Pursuing impossible goals
- **"Reality-biting"** - Harsh truth hitting hard
