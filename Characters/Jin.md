### Basic Information

- **Full Name:** Jin Nakamura
- **Aliases/Nicknames:** "Patch" (professional handle, earned from his ability to "patch" any tech problem)
- **Age:** 26
- **Gender:** Male
- **Pronouns:** He/Him
- **Species/Race:** Human
- **Ethnicity/Cultural Background:** Japanese-American, third generation slum dweller
- **Nationality/Citizenship:** Neo-Angeles Corporate Zone (technically stateless)
- **Birthplace:** South Shore Slum, Underground Tech District
- **Current Residence:** Modified storage room in Phoenix Recovery facility (sleeps next to his workshop)
- **Occupation:** Chief Engineer at Phoenix Recovery, former illegal augmentation clinic operator
- **Social Class/Status:** Underground economy → Legitimate tech professional
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Good with perfectionist tendencies
- **Affiliations/Factions:** Phoenix Recovery Inner Circle, Former Underground Augmentation Network
- **Languages Spoken:** Corporate Standard (fluent), Street Cant (native), Japanese (basic), Tech Shorthand, Machine Code
- **Religion/Beliefs:** Technology as salvation, believes engineering can solve human suffering

### Appearance
- **Height:** 5'6" (168cm)
- **Build:** Wiry and compact, optimized for crawling through tight spaces and precision work
- **Skin Tone:** Pale from years of underground work, slight yellow tint from workshop lighting
- **Hair Color & Style:** Spiky black hair, usually disheveled from running hands through it while thinking
- **Eye Color:** Dark brown, often hidden behind magnification goggles
- **Intimate parts:** Small frame but surprisingly strong hands and forearms from years of precise work
- **Distinguishing Features (Scars, Tattoos, etc.):** Multiple small scars on hands and forearms from tools and circuitry, burn mark on left palm from a plasma cutter accident, small tattoo of a circuit board pattern on right wrist
- **Preferred Clothing Style:** Oil-stained coveralls or work clothes, always wearing tool belts, fingerless gloves for dexterity
- **Augmentations/Cybernetics (if any):** Basic neural port (self-installed), enhanced vision implants for microscopic work, subdermal toolkit in left forearm

### Personality & Psychology

- **Core Personality Traits:** Perfectionist, workaholic, socially awkward but passionate, loyal to those who earn his respect
- **Strengths:** Brilliant technical mind, innovative problem-solving, absolute dedication, surprisingly brave when protecting his work or friends
- **Weaknesses:** Obsessive tendencies, poor social skills, neglects self-care, impulsive when excited about new tech
- **Fears & Traumas:** Technology failing when lives depend on it, being seen as inadequate, returning to the desperation of slum life
- **Goals & Aspirations:** Perfect the art of human-machine integration, prove himself worthy of Vyr4n's mentorship, create technology that genuinely helps people
- **Hobbies & Interests:** Collecting vintage electronics, building unnecessary but elegant gadgets, studying pre-collapse engineering texts
- **Quirks & Habits:** Hums electronic music while working, organizes tools obsessively, lives on energy drinks and nutrient bars, talks to machines as if they're alive
- **Mannerisms & Speech Patterns:** Rapid, technical speech when excited; uses engineering metaphors for everything; often distracted mid-conversation by technical problems
- **Moral Philosophy:** Technology should enhance human potential, not replace human connection; believes efficiency and beauty can coexist
- **How They View Authority/Society:** Distrusts corporate authority but respects earned expertise; sees society as a broken machine that needs better engineering
- **Greatest Regret:** An augmentation procedure that went wrong in his clinic days, leaving a client partially paralyzed
- **Greatest Joy:** The moment when a complex system comes online perfectly for the first time
- **Sexual preferences:** Gay, attracted to intelligence and competence, prefers emotional connection over casual encounters

### Abilities & Powers

- **Natural Talents:** Intuitive understanding of mechanical and electronic systems, perfect spatial reasoning, exceptional hand-eye coordination
- **Trained Skills:** Advanced cybernetic engineering, neural interface design, system architecture, improvised repair techniques, augmentation surgery
- **Combat Abilities (Melee, Firearms, Magic, etc.):** Basic self-defense, prefers non-lethal technical solutions (EMP devices, system shutdowns), surprisingly good with precision tools as weapons
- **Supernatural/Magical Abilities (if any):** None, but his technical intuition borders on supernatural
- **Technological Proficiency (Hacking, Engineering, etc.):** Master-level hardware engineering, expert-level software development, advanced hacking capabilities, pioneering augmentation techniques
- **Cybernetic Enhancements/Biotech Mods (if applicable):** Self-installed neural port for direct machine interface, enhanced vision system for microscopic work, subdermal toolkit with basic electronics
- **Weaknesses & Limitations:** Physically fragile, socially isolated, becomes obsessive about projects to his detriment, limited combat ability

### Equipment & Resources

- **Weapons:** Modified plasma cutter (doubles as weapon), EMP grenades (homemade), precision tools that can be weaponized
- **Armor/Defensive Gear:** Reinforced coveralls with built-in emergency medical systems, hardened neural port against cyber attacks
- **Vehicles:** Modified hover-bike optimized for carrying equipment, various remote drones for dangerous repair work
- **Gadgets & Tools:** Custom-built diagnostic equipment, portable manufacturing unit, advanced surgical tools, emergency repair kits
- **Personal Items of Importance:** His grandfather's mechanical watch (pre-digital era), first circuit board he ever successfully built, photo of his illegal clinic's success stories
- **Wealth & Assets:** Modest salary from Phoenix Recovery, valuable equipment and knowledge, underground reputation worth significant money
- **Allies & Contacts:** Underground augmentation network, salvage dealers, former clinic clients who are fiercely loyal

### Background & History

- **Family & Relatives:** Parents died in industrial accident when he was 12, raised by grandfather who was a pre-collapse electronics technician, no other known living relatives
- **Notable Relationships (Romantic, Platonic, Rivalries, etc.):** 
  - **Vyr4n:** Mentor figure and inspiration, student-teacher relationship
  - **Marcus "Tank" Rodriguez:** Protective older brother figure
  - **Kaia "Whisper" Okafor:** Professional collaboration, mutual respect for each other's expertise
  - **Former clinic clients:** Grateful patients who see him as a savior
- **Upbringing & Childhood:** Grew up in the technical underground of South Shore Slum, learned electronics from grandfather, started building and fixing things to survive
- **Education & Training:** Self-taught through salvaged technical manuals and trial-and-error, learned surgery through necessity and desperation
- **Significant Past Events:** 
  - Age 12: Parents' death in factory explosion
  - Age 16: Built his first successful augmentation 
  - Age 19: Opened illegal clinic
  - Age 23: Disastrous procedure that paralyzed a client
  - Age 24: Recruited by Vyr4n after impressing him with innovative repair work
- **Defining Moment(s):** The failed augmentation procedure that taught him the weight of responsibility; meeting Vyr4n and realizing his potential
- **Criminal Record (if any):** Illegal medical practice, unlicensed augmentation procedures, tech theft for clinic supplies
- **Enemies & Conflicts:** Corporate security forces who want to shut down underground clinics, rival underground engineers jealous of his skill
- **Current Status & Recent Activities:** Chief Engineer at Phoenix Recovery, developing new ethical augmentation procedures, mentoring younger technicians

### World Interaction & Themes

- **How They Fit into the Setting:** Represents the underground technical expertise that keeps the post-corporate world functioning, bridge between illegal innovation and legitimate business
- **What They Represent in the Narrative:** The idea that technology can be a force for healing rather than exploitation, redemption through constructive rather than destructive innovation
- **Themes Associated with the Character:** Redemption through expertise, the responsibility that comes with power over human enhancement, finding family in unexpected places
- **Potential Character Arcs:** 
  - Learning to balance perfectionism with pragmatism
  - Developing leadership skills as Phoenix Recovery grows
  - Confronting the consequences of his illegal clinic days
  - Finding romantic connection despite his social awkwardness
- **Conflicts They Face (Internal & External):**
  - **Internal:** Guilt over past mistakes, imposter syndrome despite obvious talent, fear of intimate relationships
  - **External:** Corporate attempts to recruit or eliminate him, pressure from underground network to return to illegal work, technical challenges that push his abilities to the limit

### Key Relationships Within Phoenix Recovery

- **Vyr4n:** Deep mentor-student bond, Vyr4n sees Jin as proof that technology can heal rather than harm
- **Inner Circle:** Valued team member whose technical expertise is essential to operations
- **Clients:** Patients who come for ethical augmentation procedures, many are former street clinic customers
