## One-Line Pitch

Former corporate enforcer turned loyal lieutenant who serves as both military strategist and moral compass to Kyr4n, struggling between father-figure devotion and watching his protégé drift toward authoritarianism.

---

## Physical Snapshot

- **Appearance:** Imposing 6'2" frame with solid muscle from decades of military training, steel gray hair cropped in military style, weathered olive skin, and alert dark brown eyes. Chrome-finish cybernetic left arm and bearing of career soldier.
- **Distinctive Features:** Jagged scar along left jawline from shrapnel, small military unit tattoo on right shoulder, cybernetic arm that whirs softly when adjusting
- **Typical Look:** Tactical clothing with armored vest during operations, simple civilian clothes off-duty, always carries wedding ring from deceased wife

---

## Personality Core

- **Primary Trait:** Unwavering protective loyalty combined with professional military discipline
- **Secondary Trait:** Quiet moral authority that questions methods while maintaining absolute reliability
- **Fatal Flaw:** Overprotective nature and tendency to shoulder responsibility for others' failures, enabling Kyr4n's authoritarian drift while trying to guide him
- **Core Motivation:** Building something meaningful and lasting while protecting those who cannot protect themselves, honoring his deceased wife's philosophy that "honor isn't about what you do, but what you refuse to do"

---

## Key Relationships

- **Kyr4n (Vyry4n):** Father-son dynamic built on Kyr4n sparing his life during Rico's takeover; serves as Head of Security and moral advisor while increasingly conflicted about authoritarian methods
- **Zara:** Professional respect and coordination as fellow inner circle member; recognizes her as perfect tactical complement
- **Dr. Elena Vasquez:** Shared concern about organization's ethical compromises; allies in attempting to maintain humanity amid efficiency
- **Deceased Wife:** Lost 5 years ago to illness; her memory and philosophy guide his moral compass

---

## Story Role

- **Narrative Function:** Provides tactical expertise, serves as Kyr4n's conscience, represents old-school military honor in criminal world
- **First Appearance:** Arc 3, Chapter 3.05 (as Rico's enforcer before joining Kyr4n)
- **Current Arc:** Arc 4 - Established as Head of Security with growing moral conflict
- **Key Scenes:** Spared by Kyr4n during takeover, establishing inner circle, territorial defense planning, witnessing Kyr4n's isolation

---

## Quick Dialogue Sample

**Casual:** "Sir, with respect, this plan has too many variables. Let me handle the details."  
**Stressed:** "Violence should be surgical—precise, necessary, over quickly."  
**Signature Line:** "You spared my life when you had every reason not to. That debt doesn't expire."

## Writer's Quick Notes

- **Voice Reminder:** Speaks deliberately with military cadence, uses "sir" and "ma'am" naturally, rarely curses, prefers tactical precision over emotional expression
- **Consistency Check:** Always sits facing the door, checks exits unconsciously, maintains weapons obsessively, drinks tea instead of coffee
- **Character in 3 Words:** Loyal | Protective | Conflicted
