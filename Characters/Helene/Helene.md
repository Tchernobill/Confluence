## One-Line Pitch

Elegant fallen aristocrat who transformed from medical school dropout and exploitation victim into Kyr4n's indispensable second-in-command, wielding maternal warmth and administrative steel with equal grace.

---

## Physical Snapshot

- **Appearance:** Graceful woman in her mid-thirties with rich auburn hair typically styled in practical elegance, warm hazel-green eyes that radiate intelligence and compassion, and aristocratic features softened by genuine warmth. Maintains impeccable posture and refined bearing despite past trauma.
- **Distinctive Features:** French-Canadian cheekbones, expressive mouth, faint marks on wrists hidden under long sleeves, perfect aristocratic grace in movement, slight calluses on fingertips from medical work
- **Typical Look:** Professional administrative attire that allows quick transition to medical work, always impeccably groomed as armor against the world, elegant but practical clothing that covers scars

---

## Personality Core

- **Primary Trait:** Nurturing administrator who leads with maternal strength and organizational precision
- **Secondary Trait:** Fiercely protective of her "organizational family" with aristocratic dignity maintained despite trauma
- **Fatal Flaw:** Tendency to mother people who don't want it; sacrifices own needs for others; perfectionist about both medical care and organizational management
- **Core Motivation:** Protect and nurture the family Kyr4n has built while ensuring operational excellence and healing those under her charge

---

## Key Relationships

- **Kyr4n:** Complex blend of maternal care, professional partnership, and suppressed romantic attraction; his trusted second-in-command and unofficial family matriarch
- **Marcus:** Professional respect, shared protective instincts, and mutual understanding as fellow members of inner circle
- **Rico (former):** Traumatic exploitation relationship that shaped her protective instincts and administrative drive
- **Organization members:** Maternal figure and respected administrator who balances compassion with operational necessity

---

## Story Role

- **Narrative Function:** Emotional anchor and administrative backbone of Kyr4n's organization; provides maternal warmth while ensuring operational efficiency; bridge between refined cultural background and harsh survival necessities
- **First Appearance:** Rico's takeover period (pre-story flashback)
- **Current Arc:** Power Era - Managing organizational operations as Chief Administrative Officer
- **Key Scenes:** First meeting Kyr4n during Rico's reign, establishing medical operations, becoming unofficial family matriarch, critical administrative decisions
