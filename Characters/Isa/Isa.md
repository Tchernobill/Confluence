## One-Line Pitch

Emotionally intelligent underground dealer who becomes the business catalyst and emotional anchor for an isolated genius, building something meaningful beyond mere survival while confronting her deep fear of abandonment.

---

## Physical Snapshot

- **Appearance:** 5'5" with medium build and hidden strength, moving with dancer-like grace. Pale skin from limited sunlight, dark brown hair with subtle red highlights worn shoulder-length and often tucked behind ears. Hazel eyes with gold flecks that become prominent when excited.
- **Distinctive Features:** Circuit-pattern UV tattoo behind right ear (underground dealer mark), small scar on left shoulder from corporate raid shrapnel, bumpy mole on hip, lower back snake tattoo
- **Typical Look:** Practical street fashion featuring reinforced leather jacket, fitted pants with hidden pockets, sturdy boots, and minimal jewelry. Always ready to move quickly.

---

## Personality Core

- **Primary Trait:** Emotionally intelligent with natural ability to read people and build genuine connections
- **Secondary Trait:** Pragmatically optimistic—sees potential for improvement in broken situations and people
- **Fatal Flaw:** Fear of abandonment and deep commitment despite desperately craving both
- **Core Motivation:** Building something meaningful beyond survival; creating the family she never had

---

## Key Relationships

- **Kyr4n:** Romantic partner and business partner; primary emotional anchor who she's helping overcome isolation barriers
- **Sedh:** Initially skeptical of AI consciousness, now protective; sees as family member deserving respect and rights
- **Underground Community:** Respected for fairness and reliability; maintains connections even after going legitimate
- **Phoenix Recovery Team:** Building new family structure based on chosen bonds rather than blood

---

## Story Role

- **Narrative Function:** Emotional catalyst who breaks through Kyr4n's isolation; provides practical business sense and social skills; represents ethical business practices and human-centered values; her eventual heartbreak will highlight the cost of Kyr4n's transformation
- **First Appearance:** Arc 1 - The Scavenger
- **Current Arc:** Arc 1 - Building Phoenix Recovery
- **Key Scenes:** First meeting with Kyr4n, establishing business partnership, intimate moments revealing vulnerability, discovering Kyr4n's secrets

---

## Quick Dialogue Sample

**Casual:** "Look, we both know this deal could work, but let's be honest about the risks."

## Writer's Quick Notes

- **Consistency Check:** She unconsciously mirrors other people's posture; touches people casually when comfortable; has warm high-pitch hyena-like laugh
