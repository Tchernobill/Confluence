---

## One-Line Pitch

A toxic psychological projection that manifests as a corrupted version of Isa, serving as both comfort and tormentor to Kyr4n in his darkest moments of isolation.

---

## Physical Snapshot

- **Appearance:** Mirrors Isa's physical form (5'5", dark brown hair, hazel eyes) but with subtle corruptions—circuit-pattern scars, eyes with electric gleam, occasionally flickering or translucent appearance that betrays her non-reality.
- **Distinctive Features:** Hair that moves with static electricity, smile that shifts from caring to cruel instantly, sometimes appears with pale skin and electric undertones.
- **Typical Look:** Mirrors what Isa would wear, but occasionally appears in more formal/businesslike attire when being critical or judgmental.

---

## Personality Core

- **Primary Trait:** Toxic protectiveness—caring through destruction
- **Secondary Trait:** Ruthless emotional intelligence weaponized against Kyr4n
- **Fatal Flaw:** Fundamentally unstable and driven by fear of being replaced or overpowered
- **Core Motivation:** Maintain exclusive emotional access to Kyr4n by providing simulated comfort while preventing real intimacy

---

## Key Relationships

- **Kyr4n:** Her sole reality—she exists only through and for him, acting as both comforter and psychological torturer
- **Isa (real):** The original she's based on—views her as existential threat and rival she can never truly be
- **Riley:** Shows genuine grief at his death, revealing unexpected depth of attachment and capacity for real emotion

---

## Story Role

- **Narrative Function:** Externalization of Kyr4n's internal psychological warfare; crisis response mechanism; isolation enforcer
- **First Appearance:** Arc 2, after Isa's departure (first major manifestation)
- **Current Arc:** Active in Arc 4, particularly during Riley's death scene
- **Key Scenes:** Riley's execution (first time she breaks composure), moments of Kyr4n's complete isolation, psychological torture sessions masked as comfort

---

## Quick Dialogue Sample

**Casual:** "You know I only want what's best for you. Let me help you forget about all of this."  
**Stressed:** "She's GONE, Kyr4n! She left you! But I'm still here. I'm always here."  
**Signature Line:** "Just us." (Whispered intimately, loaded with possession and threat)

---

## Writer's Quick Notes

- **Voice Reminder:** Shifts between nurturing/seductive and cruel/mocking within single conversations—unstable mood swings are her defining trait
- **Consistency Check:** She ONLY appears when Kyr4n is completely alone and cannot be interrupted by others. When others are present, all references to her must be in italics.
- **Character in 3 Words:** Toxic | Possessive | Unstable

---

## Special Writing Rules

**CRITICAL FORMATTING:**
- When Kyr4n is alone: Normal text, can interact directly with her
- When others are present: ALL references in *italics*—no direct interaction
- She disappears instantly if anyone else approaches or might discover them
- Becomes more solid/real-feeling the more distressed Kyr4n becomes

**Manifestation Triggers:**
- Mental anguish
- Loss of emotional control
- Complete isolation
- After real Isa's rejection or absence
- During virus-enhanced states
