### Basic Information

- **Full Name:** Mat Chang
- **Aliases/Nicknames:** "Wire" (professional handle)
- **Age:** 29
- **Gender:** Male
- **Pronouns:** He/Him
- **Species/Race:** Human
- **Ethnicity/Cultural Background:** Korean-Canadian, second generation corporate zone resident
- **Nationality/Citizenship:** Neo-Montreal Corporate Zone
- **Birthplace:** Mid-tier corporate housing district
- **Current Residence:** Converted server room in abandoned office building
- **Occupation:** Data Archaeologist / Information Broker
- **Social Class/Status:** Underground economy elite (makes good money but lives off-grid)
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral with selective moral principles
- **Affiliations/Factions:** Independent contractor, occasional Phoenix Recovery associate
- **Languages Spoken:** Corporate Standard, Korean, Data Query languages, Street Cant, Corporate Legal
- **Religion/Beliefs:** Information wants to be free, but everyone has secrets worth keeping

### Appearance
- **Height:** 5'8" (173cm)
- **Build:** Lean and wiry, moves with casual grace
- **Skin Tone:** Light olive, slightly pale from spending time indoors
- **Hair Color & Style:** Changes frequently - currently electric blue, previously black, green, silver. Always styled differently, often asymmetrical
- **Eye Color:** Dark brown, often distant or calculating
- **Distinguishing Features (Scars, Tattoos, etc.):** Small scar through left eyebrow, subtle geometric tattoos on forearms that actually function as data storage, callused fingertips from constant typing
- **Preferred Clothing Style:** Vintage band t-shirts, worn jeans, collection of different jackets and hoodies that he cycles through, always wears fingerless gloves
- **Augmentations/Cybernetics (if any):** Neural interface jack (hidden under hair), subdermal data storage implants, enhanced visual processing for reading code

### Personality & Psychology

- **Core Personality Traits:** Mysteriously aloof, sardonic wit, emotionally guarded, fiercely independent, casually brilliant
- **Strengths:** Exceptional pattern recognition, unshakeable cool under pressure, vast network of information sources, ability to find connections others miss
- **Weaknesses:** Commitment issues, tendency to disappear when things get complicated, struggles with genuine emotional intimacy, can be brutally honest
- **Fears & Traumas:** Fear of being truly known, abandonment issues from corporate family that prioritized work over relationships, terrified of being trapped or controlled
- **Goals & Aspirations:** Wants to uncover the "big truth" behind corporate information control, seeks freedom to come and go as he pleases
- **Hobbies & Interests:** Collecting vintage data storage devices, underground music scenes, urban exploration, reading deleted corporate emails like literature
- **Quirks & Habits:** Changes appearance when bored or stressed, speaks in deadpan delivery, always has three different exit strategies, quotes obscure data fragments
- **Mannerisms & Speech Patterns:** Dry, sardonic tone with occasional bursts of genuine enthusiasm about information, tends to be blunt and direct, uses technical metaphors casually
- **Moral Philosophy:** Information is power, but power should be distributed; secrets are fine but lies are not
- **How They View Authority/Society:** Deeply suspicious of any authority, believes most social structures are elaborate information control schemes
- **Greatest Regret:** Not saving important data when his first mentor was "disappeared" by corporate security
- **Greatest Joy:** The moment when a complex data puzzle finally reveals its pattern
- **Sexual preferences:** Pansexual but emotionally unavailable, tends toward casual relationships

### Abilities & Powers

- **Natural Talents:** Intuitive understanding of information systems, exceptional memory for data patterns, natural talent for reading people's digital habits
- **Trained Skills:** Advanced hacking techniques, data archaeology, social engineering, counter-surveillance, information verification
- **Combat Abilities (Melee, Firearms, Magic, etc.):** Minimal combat training, prefers to avoid physical confrontation, good at running and hiding
- **Supernatural/Magical Abilities (if any):** None
- **Technological Proficiency (Hacking, Engineering, etc.):** Elite-level data manipulation, expert in multiple programming languages, specialized in deleted data recovery
- Augmentations/Cybernetics
- **Cybernetic Enhancements/Biotech Mods (if applicable):** Neural interface allows direct brain-computer interaction, enhanced visual processing for reading multiple data streams simultaneously
- **Weaknesses & Limitations:** Physical vulnerability, tendency to get obsessed with data puzzles and ignore practical concerns, social awkwardness in non-digital contexts

### Equipment & Resources

- **Weapons:** Modified stun pistol (rarely carried), various electronic countermeasure devices
- **Armor/Defensive Gear:** Jacket with built-in signal blocking, portable EMP generator
- **Vehicles:** Modified motorcycle with stealth capabilities, prefers public transport to avoid tracking
- **Gadgets & Tools:** Custom data mining rigs, portable quantum decryption device, collection of vintage and cutting-edge storage media
- **Personal Items of Importance:** Photo of his first mentor, collection of "ghost data" from deleted corporate files, vintage music player
- **Wealth & Assets:** Significant digital currency holdings, multiple fake identities, various safe houses and data caches
- **Allies & Contacts:** Network of information brokers, some underground journalists, former corporate employees with access

### Background & History

- **Family & Relatives:** Parents work in corporate data management, relationship is distant and formal, younger sister who's a corporate lawyer (they don't talk)
- **Notable Relationships (Romantic, Platonic, Rivalries, etc.):** Series of casual relationships that ended when things got "too real," mentor figure who was disappeared, ongoing rivalry with corporate data security teams
- **Upbringing & Childhood:** Raised in sterile corporate environment, discovered hacking as escape from family expectations
- **Education & Training:** Corporate technical school (dropped out), self-taught advanced techniques, informal mentorship from underground data archaeologist
- **Significant Past Events:** Discovery of major corporate cover-up at age 19, mentor's disappearance when Riley was 23, first major data score that established his reputation
- **Defining Moment(s):** Finding evidence of his own family's involvement in data manipulation schemes, deciding to go fully underground rather than confront them
- **Criminal Record (if any):** Officially clean (good at covering tracks), unofficially wanted by multiple corporate security divisions
- **Enemies & Conflicts:** Corporate data security, former clients who didn't like what he uncovered, information brokers who see him as competition
- **Current Status & Recent Activities:** Working as independent contractor, occasional collaboration with Phoenix Recovery, building comprehensive map of corporate information control networks

### World Interaction & Themes

- **How They Fit into the Setting:** Represents the underground information economy, bridges legitimate and criminal data worlds
- **What They Represent in the Narrative:** The cost of living with too much knowledge, the loneliness of seeing through society's lies, the power and burden of information
- **Themes Associated with the Character (e.g., Redemption, Chaos vs. Order, Transhumanism):** Information as power, isolation vs. connection, the price of truth, identity in a surveilled world
- **Potential Character Arcs:** Learning to trust others with his discoveries, confronting his family about their corporate involvement, deciding whether information should be freely shared or carefully controlled
- **Conflicts They Face (Internal & External):** Balancing desire for truth with need for secrecy, managing relationships while maintaining independence, corporate forces trying to capture or eliminate him
