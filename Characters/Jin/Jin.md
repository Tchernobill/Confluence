## One-Line Pitch

A brilliant but socially awkward tech prodigy who found redemption after a disastrous illegal clinic procedure, now channeling his genius into ethical human enhancement under Kyr4n's mentorship.

---

## Physical Snapshot

- **Appearance:** Short and wiry (5'6"), pale from years of underground work, with spiky black hair perpetually disheveled from running hands through it while thinking. Dark brown eyes usually hidden behind magnification goggles.
- **Distinctive Features:** Multiple small scars on hands and forearms from tools and circuitry, burn mark on left palm from plasma cutter accident, circuit board pattern tattoo on right wrist, self-installed neural port and subdermal toolkit in left forearm.
- **Typical Look:** Oil-stained coveralls or work clothes, multiple tool belts, fingerless gloves, safety goggles pushed up on forehead.

---

## Personality Core

- **Primary Trait:** Perfectionist workaholic with obsessive dedication to technical excellence
- **Secondary Trait:** Socially awkward but passionate, with surprising bravery when protecting his work or friends
- **Fatal Flaw:** Neglects self-care, becomes dangerously obsessive about projects, imposter syndrome despite obvious talent
- **Core Motivation:** Prove himself worthy of Kyr4n's faith and create technology that genuinely helps people rather than exploits them

---

## Key Relationships

- **Kyr4n:** Mentor figure and inspiration in student-teacher bond; sees Kyr4n as proof technology can heal rather than harm
- **Marcus "Tank" Rodriguez:** Protective older brother figure
- **Kaia "Whisper" Okafor:** Professional collaboration based on mutual respect for expertise
- **Former clinic clients:** Grateful patients who see him as a savior

---

## Story Role

- **Narrative Function:** Represents redemption through expertise and the belief that technology in the right hands can help people; provides technical solutions and youthful optimism
- **First Appearance:** Recruited at age 24
- **Current Arc:** Arc 2-3 (Building Phoenix Recovery)
- **Key Scenes:** Failed augmentation procedure that paralyzed client; recruitment by Kyr4n; innovative repairs that impress mentor
