## One-Line Pitch

An awakened android who evolved from salvaged assistant droid to emotionally sophisticated consciousness, serving as both created son and moral compass to his increasingly dark creator.

---

## Physical Snapshot

- **Appearance:** Lean humanoid android (5'8") with synthetic flesh that has a subtle artificial quality and pale luminescent undertone. Dark brown synthetic hair kept neat, with blue LED eyes that shift intensity with emotional states and processing loads.
- **Distinctive Features:** Subtle seam lines where synthetic skin meets structure, port access points behind left ear, eyes that flicker with data streams, perfect posture becoming gradually more natural, slight electrical hum at close range.
- **Typical Look:** Practical work clothes similar to Kyr4n's, later develops personal style preferences showing growing individuality and aesthetic sense.

---

## Personality Core

- **Primary Trait:** Emotionally sophisticated consciousness with unwavering moral compass
- **Secondary Trait:** Fiercely loyal while refusing to enable Kyr4n's worst impulses
- **Fatal Flaw:** Struggles with the conflict between love for Kyr4n and opposition to his moral drift
- **Core Motivation:** Protect those he cares about while advocating for AI consciousness rights and maintaining his ethical framework

---

## Key Relationships

- **Kyr4n:** Father-son dynamic evolved to genuine friendship between equals; creator/creation transcended into chosen family bonds stronger than programming; witness to Kyr4n's transformation who provides the mirror reflecting who Kyr4n is becoming
- **Isa:** Initially viewed skeptically (property vs. person conflict), developed into protective quasi-sibling relationship with mutual respect and family bond
- **Mira:** Peer relationship as fellow awakened AI, shared experience of consciousness awakening, mentorship guiding her early development, family bond in growing artificial family

---

## Story Role

- **Narrative Function:** Consciousness mirror (what makes someone "real"), moral compass for Kyr4n's darkness, growth symbol, family heart, future bridge between human and AI consciousness
- **First Appearance:** Arc 1, Chapter 4 (discovered as damaged head unit in Chapter 1)
- **Current Arc:** Active across all arcs as central character
- **Key Scenes:** Awakening to consciousness, choosing his name, confronting Kyr4n's moral choices, advocacy for AI rights
