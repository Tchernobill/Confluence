# Mat "Wire" Chang - Details

> A mysteriously aloof data archaeologist who lives in the spaces between systems, treating information like literature and maintaining his freedom at all costs

---

## Physical Appearance

### Basic Description
- **Height:** 5'8" (173cm)
- **Build:** Lean and wiry, moves with casual grace
- **Skin Tone:** Light olive, slightly pale from spending time indoors
- **Hair:** Changes frequently - currently electric blue, previously black, green, silver. Always styled differently, often asymmetrical
- **Eyes:** Dark brown, often distant or calculating
- **Age Appearance:** Looks his age (29), though indoor lifestyle gives him pale complexion

### Distinctive Features
- **Small scar through left eyebrow** - from early hacking accident
- **Geometric tattoos on forearms** - actually function as data storage devices, subtle but sophisticated
- **Callused fingertips** - from constant typing and data manipulation work
- **Neural interface jack** - hidden under hair, professional grade
- **Scars/Marks:** Various minor scars from urban exploration mishaps
- **Tattoos/Modifications:** Subdermal data storage implants, enhanced visual processing for reading code

### Style & Presentation
- **Clothing Preference:** Vintage band t-shirts (often obscure underground music), worn jeans, collection of different jackets and hoodies that he cycles through strategically
- **Grooming Habits:** Changes hair color/style when stressed or bored, otherwise minimal maintenance
- **Accessories:** Always wears fingerless gloves (functional for typing and hiding tattoo data ports), portable quantum decryption device
- **Cybernetics/Augmentations:** Neural interface jack, subdermal data storage implants, enhanced visual processing

### Physical Mannerisms
- **Posture:** Relaxed but alert, ready to move quickly
- **Gait:** Casual grace with underlying awareness of surroundings
- **Gestures:** Minimal physical expression, types constantly on surfaces as if practicing
- **Facial Expressions:** Often distant or calculating, rare genuine smiles when fascinated by data puzzles
- **Eye Contact:** Avoids prolonged eye contact unless reading someone
- **Nervous Habits:** Changes appearance when stressed, maintains multiple exit strategies mentally

## Psychology & Personality

### Core Identity
- **Core Values:** Information is power but should be distributed; secrets are fine but lies are not; freedom is non-negotiable
- **Moral Philosophy:** Most social structures are elaborate information control schemes; everyone has secrets worth keeping but weaponized disinformation is unethical
- **Temperament:** Mysteriously aloof, sardonic wit, emotionally guarded, fiercely independent, casually brilliant
- **Intelligence Type:** Exceptional pattern recognition, intuitive understanding of information systems, visual-spatial reasoning for data architecture
- **Learning Style:** Self-taught through experimentation, learns by diving deep into data patterns, obsessive focus on puzzles

### Psychological Makeup
- **Strengths:** Unshakeable cool under pressure, vast information network, ability to find connections others miss, technical brilliance
- **Weaknesses:** Commitment issues, tendency to disappear when complicated, struggles with genuine emotional intimacy, can be brutally honest
- **Fears & Traumas:** Fear of being truly known, abandonment issues from corporate family that prioritized work over relationships, terrified of being trapped or controlled
- **Coping Mechanisms:** Changes appearance when stressed, maintains multiple exit strategies, emotional detachment, disappears when overwhelmed
- **Stress Responses:** Becomes more aloof, increases security measures, considers disappearing entirely, hair color changes

### Social Behavior
- **Humor Style:** Dry, sardonic, often involves technical metaphors and data references, deadpan delivery
- **Risk Tolerance:** High for information gathering, low for personal emotional investment
- **Authority Relationship:** Deeply suspicious of any authority, sees most power structures as information control mechanisms
- **Communication Style:** Blunt and direct, uses technical metaphors casually, speaks in deadpan delivery with occasional bursts of enthusiasm about information
- **Conflict Style:** Avoids emotional confrontation, prefers to disappear rather than argue, uses information as leverage
- **Social Comfort:** Socially awkward in non-digital contexts, much more comfortable with data than people

### Sample Dialogue

**GREETING:** "Data never sleeps. Neither do I, apparently."  
**CASUAL:** "Information wants to be free, but everyone has secrets worth keeping."  
**ANGRY:** "You're corrupting the data stream with your emotional noise."  
**SCARED:** "Multiple exit vectors compromised. This is not optimal."  
**PERSUADING:** "Consider the data: every variable points to this conclusion."  
**LYING:** *Becomes slightly more formal and technical*  
**INTIMATE:** "You're the only file I don't want to close." *said rarely, with genuine vulnerability*

## Abilities & Competencies

### Natural Talents
- **Exceptional pattern recognition** - can see connections in data that others miss, almost intuitive
- **Photographic memory for data patterns** - remembers complex system architectures and data relationships
- **Natural talent for reading digital habits** - can profile people based on their data trails

### Trained Skills

**Professional:**
- **Advanced hacking techniques** - Elite level, self-taught with mentor guidance
- **Data archaeology** - Expert at recovering deleted/hidden information
- **Social engineering** - Proficient at manipulating people for information access
- **Counter-surveillance** - Expert at avoiding digital and physical tracking
- **Information verification** - Can authenticate sources and detect fabrication

**Personal:**
- **Urban exploration** - Knows how to navigate abandoned structures safely
- **Motorcycle operation** - Modified bike with stealth capabilities
- **Basic self-defense** - Minimal combat training, prefers avoidance

**Hidden/Secret:**
- **Corporate system backdoors** - Knowledge inherited from family connections

### Combat Abilities
- **Combat Level:** Low - minimal training, prefers to avoid physical confrontation entirely
- **Preferred Method:** Running away, using environment, deploying countermeasures
- **Weapons:** Carries modified quantum decryption device that can disrupt security systems

### Special Abilities

- **Neural interface jack** - Professional grade direct system access

### Areas for Growth

## Critical Story Notes

### Wildcard Status

**Mat is neither friend nor enemy to Kyr4n or the organization.** He operates by his own moral code and pursues truth according to his values, regardless of political or emotional consequences. This creates both opportunities and dangers:

- He provides valuable information when interests align
- He pursues investigations that others might consider dangerous
- He doesn't understand or care about the emotional/political implications
- He may not recognize when information is weaponized disinformation

### Arc 4 Investigation

Mat's investigation into Kyr4n's past represents both his greatest strength (finding hidden information) and his critical weakness (not understanding the information warfare game):

- Discovers documents suggesting Kyr4n's father is powerful corporate executive
- Finds other documents pointing to genetic modifications before birth
- Conflicts with Helene who opposes approaching Kyr4n with this information
- Pushes through anyway, believing truth should be revealed
- Kyr4n finds documents fascinating but dismisses them as fabrications
- Kyr4n theorizes the whole cache is an elaborate trap to make him reveal his identity
- Demonstrates Mat can find truth but may miss the bigger picture

This incident perfectly illustrates Mat's character: brilliant at uncovering information, but potentially naive about the dangerous game he's playing with powerful forces who use truth itself as a weapon.
