### Basic Information

- **Full Name:** Mark Chen
- **Aliases/Nicknames:** "Chief" (professional), "Tank" (old military buddies)
- **Age:** 48
- **Gender:** Male
- **Pronouns:** He/Him
- **Species/Race:** Human
- **Ethnicity/Cultural Background:** Chinese-American, second generation
- **Nationality/Citizenship:** Neo-Angeles Corporate Zone
- **Birthplace:** Military Base Delta-7, Colorado Sector
- **Current Residence:** Mid-tier corporate housing, Security District
- **Occupation:** Senior Security Chief
- **Social Class/Status:** Corporate middle management
- **Alignment:** Lawful Good with pragmatic flexibility
- **Affiliations/Factions:** Phoenix Recovery (current), Former Corporate Security Division
- **Languages Spoken:** Corporate Standard (native), Mandarin (conversational), Military Cant, Security Protocols
- **Religion/Beliefs:** Practical Buddhism, believes in earned loyalty and protecting those who can't protect themselves

### Appearance
- **Height:** 6'2" (188cm)
- **Build:** Solid, muscular frame with the bearing of a career soldier
- **Skin Tone:** Light olive, weathered from years of outdoor security work
- **Hair Color & Style:** Steel gray, cropped short in military style, receding at temples
- **Eye Color:** Dark brown with alert intensity
- **Distinguishing Features (Scars, Tattoos, etc.):** Jagged scar along left jawline from shrapnel, small tattoo of unit insignia on right shoulder, hands show calluses from decades of weapons training
- **Preferred Clothing Style:** Practical tactical gear during work, simple civilian clothes off-duty
- **Augmentations/Cybernetics (if any):** Subdermal armor weave in torso (barely visible), enhanced reflexes via spinal neural interface (no external hardware visible)

### Personality & Psychology

- **Core Personality Traits:** Protective, methodical, quietly observant, unshakeable loyalty once earned
- **Strengths:** Strategic thinking under pressure, ability to read people and situations, natural leadership, unwavering moral compass
- **Weaknesses:** Tends to shoulder responsibility for others' failures, struggles with authority figures who lack field experience, can be overprotective
- **Fears & Traumas:** Fear of failing to protect those under his care, haunted by military casualties under his command
- **Goals & Aspirations:** Wants to build something lasting and meaningful, hopes to mentor the next generation of security professionals
- **Hobbies & Interests:** Classical music, chess, maintaining vintage firearms, reading military history
- **Quirks & Habits:** Always sits facing the door, unconsciously checks exits in new spaces, drums fingers when thinking
- **Mannerisms & Speech Patterns:** Speaks deliberately with slight military cadence, uses "sir" and "ma'am" naturally, rarely uses profanity
- **Moral Philosophy:** Believes in protecting the innocent and that strength should serve those who lack it
- **How They View Authority/Society:** Respects competent leadership, skeptical of authority that hasn't proven itself in field conditions
- **Greatest Regret:** Losing three junior officers in a corporate raid due to faulty intelligence
- **Greatest Joy:** Seeing former trainees succeed and protect others
- **Sexual preferences:** Heterosexual, monogamous

### Abilities & Powers

- **Natural Talents:** Exceptional situational awareness, instinctive tactical assessment, natural leadership presence
- **Trained Skills:** Advanced firearms proficiency, hand-to-hand combat (multiple disciplines), tactical planning, personnel management, threat assessment
- **Combat Abilities (Melee, Firearms, Magic, etc.):** Expert marksman, close quarters combat specialist, proficient with various weapons systems
- **Supernatural/Magical Abilities (if any):** None
- **Technological Proficiency (Hacking, Engineering, etc.):** Security systems expert, moderate electronics knowledge, familiar with most corporate tech
- **Cybernetic Enhancements/Biotech Mods (if applicable):** Subdermal armor provides damage resistance, neural interface enhances reaction time by 15%
- **Weaknesses & Limitations:** Limited tech skills beyond security applications, reflexes slightly slowed by age despite enhancements

### Equipment & Resources

- **Weapons:** Modified tactical pistol with smart-targeting, ceramic knife, compact rifle for serious threats
- **Armor/Defensive Gear:** Tactical vest with trauma plates, lightweight helmet with HUD display
- **Vehicles:** Armored security transport (company), personal civilian cruiser
- **Gadgets & Tools:** Security scanner suite, encrypted communicator, portable barrier generator
- **Personal Items of Importance:** Wedding ring (deceased wife), photo of former unit, chess set from his father
- **Wealth & Assets:** Moderate corporate pension, small apartment paid off, emergency savings
- **Allies & Contacts:** Network of former military and security professionals, contacts in law enforcement

### Background & History

- **Family & Relatives:** Parents deceased, younger sister (teacher in civilian sector), no children
- **Notable Relationships (Romantic, Platonic, Rivalries, etc.):** Widowed after 12 years of marriage, maintains friendships with former unit members, mentor relationship with several junior security officers
- **Upbringing & Childhood:** Raised on military bases, learned discipline and service from early age
- **Education & Training:** Military academy, specialized security training, ongoing tactical education
- **Significant Past Events:** Military service (8 years), transition to corporate security (15 years), wife's death from illness (5 years ago)
- **Defining Moment(s):** Losing soldiers under his command taught him the weight of responsibility; wife's death made him value every relationship
- **Criminal Record (if any):** Clean record, commendations for service
- **Enemies & Conflicts:** Various criminal elements he's apprehended, potential corporate rivals
- **Current Status & Recent Activities:** Recently joined Phoenix Recovery, adapting to new mission parameters focused on protection rather than enforcement

### World Interaction & Themes

- **How They Fit into the Setting:** Represents the professional military/security class that bridges corporate and street worlds
- **What They Represent in the Narrative:** The reliable foundation, competent authority that can be trusted, old-school values in a changing world
- **Themes Associated with the Character (e.g., Redemption, Chaos vs. Order, Transhumanism):** Duty vs. morality, the cost of loyalty, finding purpose after loss
- **Potential Character Arcs:** Learning to work with unconventional methods (Vyr4n's approach), discovering there are battles worth fighting beyond corporate interests, building new family bonds with the team
- **Conflicts They Face (Internal & External):** Balancing protective instincts with respect for others' autonomy, adapting to Phoenix Recovery's different mission, processing grief and finding new purpose
