# The Market

## Scene Information

- **Summary**: Kyr4n arrives at the South Shore Central Market and sets up his modest repair booth in his usual corner location. Throughout the day, he handles a few clients—from simple data pad repairs to complex neural interface calibrations—demonstrating the exceptional technical skills that have earned him "The Artisan" nickname among underground dealers. He interacts with regular vendors including Marcus (a fence) and Dr. Nyx, establishing his place in the market's social ecosystem while maintaining the careful low profile necessary for survival.

- **POV Character(s)**: Kyr4n

- **Timeline**: 2108-05-01 (Wednesday), 09:30-16:00 (after Journey to Work)

- **Location(s)**: South Shore Central Market , Kyr4n's corner stall between support pillars

- **Key Events**:
  - Kyr4n arrives and sets up portable workstation in his usual corner spot
  - First client: nervous woman with flickering data pad (routine repair)
  - Few clients throughout the day: implant calibrations, communication device repairs, targeting system modifications
  - Brief interaction with Marcus (fence) during mid-morning lull
  - More complex repair work in early afternoon
  - End of market day: packs up with enough credits for week's expenses plus small margin
  - Prepares to leave for scavenging run (leads to Scene The Tomb)

- **Foreshadowing Events**:
  - "The Artisan" reputation being established through word-of-mouth, setting up future business opportunities
  - His technical mastery hints at natural technical capabilities
  - Careful balance between visibility (enough to get clients) and invisibility (avoid becoming target) foreshadows constant tension throughout Era 1
  - The corner alcove position will be where Isa first observes him work (Scene 1.02.002)
  - The modest margin above survival expenses represents the knife-edge economic existence that makes opportunities (and risks) so tempting

- **Character Developments**:
  - Demonstrates Kyr4n's technical expertise and problem-solving methodology
  - Shows his professional competence contrasting with social awkwardness
  - Reveals his careful risk management and survival-focused thinking
  - Establishes his modest but growing reputation in underground economy
  - Shows him capable of basic social interactions when they're transactional/professional

  - Comfortable in his element (technical work) despite isolation in personal life
  - His comfort with transactional interactions but clear discomfort with anything personal establishes the emotional barriers that will need to overcome

- **Plot Advances**:

  - Introduces underground market as crucial location
  - Sets up vendor community and market ecosystem
  - Shows sustainable survival strategy

- **Objects/Items Introduced**:

## The Events

- Kyr4n arrives at underground market after tunnel journey
- Sets up portable workstation in his usual corner alcove between support pillars
- Positions hand-lettered sign and displays examples of previous work
- First client arrives: nervous woman with damaged data pad (flickering screen)
- Performs routine repair: loose display cable connection, five minutes work
- Receives payment of 50ml fresh water (easy money)
- Second client: implant calibration for enhancement software drift
- Third client: communication device repair for underground dealer
- Fourth client: targeting system modification for security guard with substandard equipment
- Mid-morning lull: brief conversation with Marcus (fence) passing by stall
- Marcus mentions recent shipment of quality components, offers Kyr4n first look
- More repair work through late morning: neural interface recalibration begins
- Early afternoon: Dr. Nyx stops by, discusses market conditions and corporate surveillance increase
- Continues work on complex neural interface requiring careful adjustment
- Additional clients throughout afternoon with various repair needs
- Each interaction follows pattern: assessment, estimate, careful work, untraceable payment
- "The Artisan" reputation reinforced through satisfied clients
- By end of day: several successful repairs, two ongoing projects requiring additional components
- Earns enough credits for week's basic expenses plus small margin for opportunities
- Packs up portable workstation efficiently
- Exchanges nods with regular vendors as market begins to close
- Prepares to leave for scavenging run in abandoned industrial district
