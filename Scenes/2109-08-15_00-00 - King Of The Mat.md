**One-Line Summary:** Kyr4n hosts an open tournament challenging his organization members to remove him from the gym mat, with pool access as stakes and his reputation on the line.

**Scene Stakes:** 
- For Kyr4n: His reputation and authority if he loses
- For Organization: Access to restored basement pool and bragging rights

- For Marcus/Team: Testing themselves against enhanced abilities
