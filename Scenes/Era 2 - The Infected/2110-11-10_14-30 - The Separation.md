## üí≠ Themes & Symbolism

### Thematic Exploration
**Primary Theme:** Limits of Love - relationship destroyed despite mutual devotion and understanding
**How It's Explored:** Through the gap between intellectual understanding (virus caused this) and emotional reality (can't overcome conditioning)

**Secondary Themes:** 
- Ambiguous Endings: not all separations are dramatic
- Unspoken Truths: what's left unsaid more powerful than declarations
- Technology's Cost: psychological damage as permanent as physical

### Symbolic Elements
- **"For now":** Symbol of comfortable lie; represents inability to accept finality
- **Business arrangements:** Represents reduction of intimate relationship to practical transactions
- **Sedh's observation:** AI understanding human emotion symbolizes knowledge without experience

### Philosophical Questions Raised
- Is love enough when psychological damage makes proximity impossible?
- Can intellectual understanding overcome emotional conditioning?
- What constitutes abandonment vs. self-preservation?
- How do we end relationships with people we still love?
