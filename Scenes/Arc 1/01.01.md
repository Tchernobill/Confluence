# Chapter 1: The Artisan's World

## Chapter Information

- **Chapter Title:** The Artisan's World
- **Arc:** Arc 1 - The Scavenger
- **Chapter Number:** 1 of 6
- **Summary:** Introduction to Vyr4n's isolated existence as a scavenger and underground repair specialist. Establishes his daily routine, hidden sanctuary, technical skills, and reputation as "The Artisan" while showing his profound loneliness and defensive barriers.
- **POV Character(s):** Vyr4n (primary)
- **Timeline:** 2108 - Vyr4n age 22, six months after his mother's death
- **Location(s):** Ventilation shaft hideout → South Shore Slum underground market
- **Key Events:** Morning routine in sanctuary, market setup, establishing reputation through skilled repairs, minor corporate harassment, return to isolation
- **Character Developments:** Establishes Vyr4n's technical mastery, survival skills, emotional barriers, and growing reputation
- **Plot Advances:** Introduces protagonist's current situation and capabilities, sets up market location and client base
- **Objects/Items Introduced:** Mother's technical journals, molecular repair chamber, market stall equipment, ventilation shaft sanctuary
- **Chapter Goal:** Establish status quo that will be disrupted by Isa's arrival
- **Chapter Mood/Tone:** Melancholic isolation transitioning to focused competence, cyberpunk atmosphere
- **Transition to Next Chapter:** Vyr4n's growing reputation attracts Isa's investigation
