## üé≠ Scene Dynamics

### Point of View Notes
**POV Character:** Third person limited, primarily following Zara Al-Rashid's perspective
**POV Limitations:** Cannot access Kyr4n's internal understanding of his abilities (he doesn't fully understand them himself); doesn't know what Marcus knows about the takeover
**Bias/Perspective:** Views everything through professional assassin/bodyguard lens; enhanced senses provide tactical data but miss the viral nature of Kyr4n's abilities
**Internal Conflict:** Professional assessment says "easy target" but evidence suggests otherwise; curiosity versus accepting mystery

### Character Objectives & Obstacles
**Zara:**
- Want: Assess client's capabilities and begin teaching basic defense
- Need: Understand what she's actually protecting to do her job effectively
- Obstacle: Kyr4n's abilities don't match any category she recognizes; enhanced senses can't detect the source of his capabilities
- Tactics: Systematic testing, escalating complexity, direct questions

**Kyr4n:**
- Want: Learn proper fighting techniques to appear less vulnerable
- Need: Protect his secret while still benefiting from training
- Obstacle: Doesn't understand why his body responds the way it does; can't explain abilities he doesn't comprehend
- Tactics: Honest cooperation, analytical approach, admitting ignorance when genuine

**Marcus:**
- Want: Let Zara discover Kyr4n's nature organically
- Need: Ensure Zara stays committed to the job
- Obstacle: Can't reveal too much without breaking Kyr4n's trust
- Tactics: Amused observation, vague confirmations, gentle deflection

### Relationship Dynamics in This Scene
- **Zara/Kyr4n:** Professional instructor/student dynamic with undercurrent of mutual curiosity; Zara assessing, Kyr4n cooperating; no personal intimacy yet but seeds of respect
- **Power Balance:** Zara has technical knowledge and physical capability, but Kyr4n has hidden advantages neither fully understands; shifts subtly as Zara realizes her assumptions are wrong
- **Subtext:** Zara thinking "what am I missing?"; Kyr4n genuinely uncertain about his own abilities; Marcus knowing more than he says
