## Core Principle

**Scene length does not equal emotional impact.**  
Emotional impact comes from **change per word**, not raw word count.

Think of scenes on two axes:

- **Length** (word count)
    
- **Emotional Delta** (how much the character _changes_)
    

A short scene can devastate.  
A long scene can feel flat if the emotional delta is small.

## Diagnostic Tool (Use This While Editing)

Ask these 5 questions:

1. What is the **emotional starting state**?
    
2. What is the **ending state**?
    
3. Where does the **irreversible moment** occur?
    
4. Does anything **after that moment** add new information?
    
5. If I cut 20%, does the emotional arc break?
