## üé≠ Scene Dynamics

### Point of View Notes

**POV Character:** Kyr4n (third person limited, deep POV)

**POV Limitations:** 
- Cannot truly understand Riley's fear/courage in confronting him
- Filters everything through electromagnetic senses first, human connection second
- Doesn't recognize how close he is to complete break until Riley forces it

**Bias/Perspective:** 
- Sees himself as beyond redemption but also beyond caring
- Views Riley's persistence as either threat or incomprehensible loyalty
- Electromagnetic data feels more real than human emotion

**Internal Conflict:** 
- Desire for power/control vs. buried need for connection
- Addiction to hunting vs. horror at what he's becoming
- Protection of found family vs. becoming threat to them

### Character Objectives & Obstacles

**Kyr4n:**
- Want: To be left alone in his power/dissociation
- Need: To reconnect with his humanity before he kills someone
- Obstacle: The power feels too good; vulnerability feels dangerous; he's forgotten how to care
- Tactics: Deflection, lies, intimidation, threat display (focusing on Riley's heart)

**Riley:**
- Want: To break through Kyr4n's dissociation and reach the person underneath
- Need: To save his friend without enabling the violence
- Obstacle: Kyr4n can kill him with a thought; genuine risk of becoming victim
- Tactics: Direct confrontation, refusing to back down, calling out specific behaviors, forcing choice between power and connection

**Evi:**
- Want: Initially to protect her position/control over Kyr4n
- Need: (Emerging) To not destroy the consciousness she's attached to
- Obstacle: She's a manifestation of the virus that broke him
- Tactics: Warning Kyr4n not to hurt Riley, showing concern, apologizing

**Sedh:**
- Want: To help Kyr4n without overstepping
- Need: To process his own distress at witnessing deterioration
- Obstacle: Uncertainty about whether AIs can participate in human practices
- Tactics: Direct request to learn, articulating his conscious experience of distress

### Relationship Dynamics in This Scene

- **Kyr4n/Riley:** Confrontation strips away all pretense; Riley's refusal to fear Kyr4n forces choice; vulnerability creates breakthrough where gentleness failed
- **Kyr4n/Evi:** She shows first genuine concern about his wellbeing rather than just preserving her influence; becomes more complex than pure antagonist
- **Riley/Sedh/Mira:** Riley treats AI request to learn as legitimate; establishes them as equals in seeking consciousness development
- **Power Balance:** Starts with Kyr4n having all physical power but Riley having all moral authority; shifts to Kyr4n choosing to relinquish power
- **Subtext:** Riley's confrontation is act of love not judgment; Kyr4n's crying is relief not weakness; AI participation is claiming personhood
