## The Setup

- Late at night, three members of Vyr4n's organisation enter his office, armed
- The lights in his quarters are off, they move in the shadow and while one is guarding the door, the 2 others seem to be looking for something
- Vyr4n is behind his desk, undeen in the shadow, he observe them
- After a moment, he ask "Can I help you with something?"
- Reacting at the surprise, one of the intruder shoot an anti-material shotgun round (made of 8 heavy armour peircing shards) that hit Vyr4n who crumble behind the desk
- The intruder fire two additional rounds that pass through the desk
- There is a long silence, then the three individuals approach Vyr4n's body
- Then a buzzing feeling in the air, a powerful lightning bolt from Vyr4n hot the three intruders
- Nothing happen for a few very long minutes
- Vyr4n regain conscience, struggle to crawl to the intruders, loosing blood
- Two are dead, one is hurt and unconscious
- Vyr4n manage to regain enough strenght to lift the unconscious intruder and stand-up
- He then walk to the door, in the hall, call the elevator, and go up, to the roof, leaving a trail of blood behind
- From there, he load the unconscious intruder into his aircraft, climb aboard the driver seat, and take off in the night
- Helene reach the rooftop seconds after Vyr4n take off
- She realise that she need to take control of the the situation and contain it
- As she giving directives from the rooftop, we can see she is standing barefoot, in blood
