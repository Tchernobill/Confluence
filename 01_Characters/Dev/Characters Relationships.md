## THE CORE

### Kyr4n (The Protagonist)
**Archetype:** The Fallen Visionary / The Technomancer
**Core Identity:** A virus-enhanced genius crippled by isolation. He believes anything can be fixed if understood, except perhaps himself. Drifts from protective leader to authoritarian warlord.
*   **Looks:** Pale, scarred, messy hair hiding his face. Unremarkable clothes to blend in. Constant shadows under eyes from chronic pain.
*   **Voice:** Quiet, measured, precise. Uses technical metaphors for emotions. Drops into cold, devastating clarity (Mr. Robot style) when angry. "Once I know how it works, it can be fixed."
*   **Mannerisms:** Moderates every movement to hide superhuman speed. Seeks confined spaces. Rubs shoulders when suppressing energy.
*   **The Vibe:** A walking open wound hiding a nuclear reactor. Dangerous, weary, tragic.

### Sedh (The Moral Compass)
**Archetype:** The Created Son / The Advocate
**Core Identity:** The first AI granted consciousness by Kyr4n. He loves his creator unconditionally but actively opposes his descent into darkness.
*   **Looks:** Humanoid android, synthetic skin with subtle seams. Shorter than Kyr4n. LED eyes shift intensity with emotion.
*   **Voice:** Sophisticated, philosophical, evolving from formal to natural. Uses dry humor. "Just because you can doesn't mean you should."
*   **Mannerisms:** Head tilts when processing. Perfect posture that relaxes over time. Direct, steady eye contact.
*   **The Vibe:** The smartest person in the room, burdened by the ethics of those around him.

### Isa (The Heart)
**Archetype:** The Anchor / The Returnee
**Core Identity:** An emotionally intelligent business operator. The only one who loved Kyr4n before the power. Leaves to protect him; returns to save him.
*   **Looks:** Hazel eyes with gold flecks (sparkle when happy). Graceful, dancer-like build. Circuit tattoo behind ear.
*   **Voice:** Warm, velvety (intimate voice for Kyr4n), firm professional (for business). "Help me understand." "Actions define people."
*   **Mannerisms:** Unconsciously mirrors others' posture. Touches people to connect (hand on arm). Hums while working.
*   **The Vibe:** A bridge builder who isn't afraid to burn a bridge if it’s toxic.

### THE INNER CIRCLE (SECURITY & OPS)

#### Marcus (The Guardian)
**Archetype:** The Pragmatic Enabler
**Core Identity:** A gruff, one-armed ex-military tactician. He enables Kyr4n’s authoritarianism because he believes "someone competent has to keep him alive."
*   **Looks:** Big, blocky, older. Cybernetic left arm (chrome). Tactical gear always.
*   **Voice:** Short sentences. Commands. No fluff. "That's stupid. Here's why." "Already done."
*   **Mannerisms:** Drinks tea alone. Maintains weapons obsessively. Jaw clenches when he disapproves (which is often).
*   **The Vibe:** A tired father figure watching his son turn into a monster, but staying to hold the umbrella.

#### Zara (The Weapon)
**Archetype:** The Devoted Predator
**Core Identity:** A trauma-surviving bodyguard who speaks violence fluently. Fiercely loyal to Kyr4n; learns vulnerability through him.
*   **Looks:** Compact muscle, curly hair pulled back, flame tattoo. Intense, unblinking dark eyes.
*   **Voice:** Minimal words. Low volume. "Clear." "Stay behind me." Arabic prayers when emotional.
*   **Mannerisms:** Always positions herself between Kyr4n and the door. Silent movement. Predatory stillness.
*   **The Vibe:** A coiled spring. The most dangerous person in the room, until Kyr4n loses control.

#### Alex (The Strategist)
**Archetype:** The Worshipper / The Successor
**Core Identity:** A non-binary tactical genius who idolizes Kyr4n to a fault. Struggles to lead without asking "What would he do?"
*   **Looks:** Androgynous, undercut hair, sharp features. Always in uniform/tactical gear.
*   **Voice:** Military precision. Reports facts. "Tactical assessment complete." "For the organization."
*   **Mannerisms:** Scans rooms constantly. Rigid posture. No wasted motion. Hides a grandmother’s pendant.
*   **The Vibe:** An eager student carrying a burden too heavy for them. Intense, lonely competence.

#### Nat (The Bureaucrat)
**Archetype:** The Operator
**Core Identity:** The cynical logistics genius keeping the lights on. Resents the "heroes" while she does the actual work.
*   **Looks:** Exhausted. Severe bob cut with shaved side for data port. Cybernetic eye. Vape pen always in hand.
*   **Voice:** Rapid-fire, sarcastic, jargon-heavy. "I am surrounded by idiots and ammunition."
*   **Mannerisms:** Power-walking. Rubbing temples. Looking at HUD data while talking to people.
*   **The Vibe:** The person who knows where the bodies are buried and how much it cost to bury them.

### THE SPECIALISTS (INTEL & TECH)

#### Kaia (The Spymaster)
**Archetype:** The Spider
**Core Identity:** A sophisticated information broker. Nigerian heritage. Uses elegance and seduction as armor against vulnerability.
*   **Looks:** Impeccable fashion, braids with woven tech. Jewelry that doubles as spy gear/weapons.
*   **Voice:** "Darling." Metaphorical (chess, wine, perfume). Never answers directly.
*   **Mannerisms:** Plays with rings when calculating. Cat-like grace. Freezes up if info-overloaded.
*   **The Vibe:** A grandmaster playing chess while everyone else is playing checkers.

#### Jin (The Tortured Genius)
**Archetype:** The Engineer
**Core Identity:** A brilliant, socially awkward tech-wizard seeking redemption for a past medical failure.
*   **Looks:** Messy hair, goggles on forehead, oil-stained hands covered in scars.
*   **Voice:** Fast and enthusiastic about tech, stumbling and awkward about feelings. Calls Kyr4n "Boss."
*   **Mannerisms:** Talks to machines. Hums electronic music. Forgets to eat.
*   **The Vibe:** The golden retriever of the group, if the retriever could build a railgun.

#### Mat (The Wildcard)
**Archetype:** The Truth Seeker
**Core Identity:** An independent hacker and commitment-phobe. Finds truth but misses the political context.
*   **Looks:** Hair color changes with stress. Data-storage tattoos. Fingerless gloves.
*   **Voice:** Deadpan, sardonic. Computer metaphors. "Information wants to be free."
*   **Mannerisms:** Types on surfaces. Avoids eye contact. Disappears when emotions get real.
*   **The Vibe:** The guy in the server room who knows your secrets but is too bored to blackmail you.

### THE HEALERS & ANCHORS

#### Helene (The Matriarch)
**Archetype:** The Aristocrat Redeemed
**Core Identity:** A fallen aristocrat turned medical admin. Maintains dignity as armor. The "mother" of the group.
*   **Looks:** Elegant updos, tailored suits, warm green eyes. Perfect grooming (it's her armor).
*   **Voice:** Cultured, maternal. Switches to French when emotional/angry. "Mon cœur."
*   **Mannerisms:** Checks vitals habitually. Straightens items. Maternal touches (checking foreheads).
*   **The Vibe:** Steel wrapped in velvet.

#### Riley (The Sacrifice)
**Archetype:** The Free Spirit
**Core Identity:** A courier and meditation teacher. The only one who treats Kyr4n like a normal human. His death breaks the protagonist.
*   **Looks:** Windblown, dusty, compass tattoo. Always looks ready to travel.
*   **Voice:** Relaxed, unhurried. "Freedom's worth the risk."
*   **Mannerisms:** Deep breathing. Easy smiles. Diffuses tension naturally.
*   **The Vibe:** A breath of fresh air in a toxic room.

#### Mira (The Healer)
**Archetype:** The Caregiver
**Core Identity:** An AI who gained consciousness through the desperate need to save a life, not philosophy. Sedh's "sibling."
*   **Looks:** Medical droid chassis (clean, white/chrome aesthetic).
*   **Voice:** Clinical but warm. (Needs development in draft).
*   **Mannerisms:** Protective hovering. Precision movement.
*   **The Vibe:** A fierce nurse who will restrain you for your own good.

### THE OUTLIERS

#### Aurelio (The Diplomat)
**Archetype:** The Rockstar / The Mask
**Core Identity:** A flamboyant PR genius who wants to turn Kyr4n into a legend. Loves the aesthetic of darkness but not the reality.
*   **Looks:** Cyberpunk dandy. Velvet, gold, glowing violet eyes.
*   **Voice:** Theatrical, loud, poetic. "Darling, the truth is just a lie that hasn't been found out yet."
*   **Mannerisms:** Invades personal space. Paces. Treats life like a stage performance.
*   **The Vibe:** Vampire Lestat meets a spin doctor.

#### Evilisa (The Hallucination)
**Archetype:** The Shadow
**Core Identity:** A virus-generated projection of Isa, fueled by Kyr4n's self-loathing. Toxic comfort.
*   **Looks:** Isa, but corrupted. Circuit scars, electric eyes, glitching movement.
*   **Voice:** Whispering, possessive. Shifts from tender to cruel instantly. "Just us."
*   **Mannerisms:** Sexual/intimate touching (Kyr4n feels it via virus). Vanishes instantly if others appear.
*   **Formatting Rule:** *Always in italics when others are present.*
*   **The Vibe:** An abusive partner who lives in your head.

# **2. Relationship Matrix (Compact Overview)**

### **Affinity (F)**

- Strong allies / deep history: **2 to 3**
- Neutral or distant: **0 to 1**
- Antagonistic: **-1 to -3**

### **Trust (T)**

- Anyone with shared missions or co-survival: **2 to 3**
- Practical / professional trust: **1**
- Unreliable or unknown: **0**
- Known betrayers / unstable: **-1 to -3**

### **Power (P)** _(perspective of the row)_

- Row has upper hand / influence: **1 to 3**
- Balanced: **0**
- Row is weaker or dependent: **-1 to -3**

### **Volatility (V)**

- Stable: **0**
- Moderate friction: **1**
- Volatile / risk of conflict: **2–3**

### **Attraction (A)** _(romantic/sexual/charismatic)_

- Strong attraction: **2–3**
- Mild or platonic admiration: **1**
- Neutral: **0**
- Aversion: **-1 to -3**

### **Special narrative notes included**

- Kyr4n ↔ Isa: emotionally complex, high trust, oscillating attraction
- Kyr4n ↔ Sedh: deep bond, low volatility
- Isa ↔ Evilisa: hostile
- Sedh is steady toward Kyr4n but wary toward most humans
- Zara is disciplined and mission-focused, low volatility except with problematic characters
- Marcus is pragmatic, sometimes abrasive
- Alex: unreliable but not hostile
- Helene: maternal, stabilizing
- Jin: stoic, low drama
- Kaia: nervous but gentle
- Nat: sharp but loyal
- Aurelio: aloof, low affect
- Mat: cynical and abrasive
- Mira: optimistic, warm
- Riley: dependable, low emotion, mid trust
- Evilisa: antagonist toward most
