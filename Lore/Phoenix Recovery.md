# Phoenix Recovery - Technical Services

## Executive Summary

Phoenix Recovery operates as a specialized electronics repair and technical services business in the South Shore Slum. Founded in 2108 by Kyr4n "The Artisan" and his partners Isa and Sedh, the company has built a reputation for achieving seemingly impossible repairs and modifications. Operating from a modest three-container facility on the south-east outskirt of the slum, Phoenix Recovery serves both legitimate clients and occasional underground customers requiring discrete technical services.

---

## Company Origins & History

### Early Operations (2102-2108)

Phoenix Recovery began as Kyr4n's individual repair services at the underground grey markets. Operating from a portable workstation at vendor stalls, Kyr4n built his reputation as "The Artisan" through exceptional technical capabilities that often salvaged electronics others deemed irreparable.

**Foundation Timeline:**

- **2102-2107**: Individual operations at grey market stalls, building client base and reputation
- **2103**: Partnership established with Isa, providing business connections and market knowledge
- **2106**: Addition of Sedh, an advanced android providing computational support and system analysis
- **2108**: Official business establishment and relocation to permanent facility

### Business Growth (2108+)

The transition from market stall to permanent facility marked Phoenix Recovery's evolution into a recognized technical service provider. The three-container complex provides dedicated workspace for precision repairs, fabrication capabilities, and discrete consultation services.

**Facility Development:**

- **Container 1**: Primary repair workshop with molecular-level stations and precision measurement equipment
- **Container 2**: Fabrication laboratory housing CNC machines, injection molding equipment, composite material workspace, and 3D printing systems
- **Container 3**: Client consultation, parts storage, administrative operations, and traditional machining tools
